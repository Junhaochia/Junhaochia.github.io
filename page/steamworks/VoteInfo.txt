<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<div class="deprecatedfunc"><span>This feature is deprecated.</span><br />
You should avoid using it as it may be removed in a future version.<br /><br />Use data <a href="/page/Category_colon_steamworks" title="Category:steamworks">steamworks</a>.<a href="/page/steamworks/FileInfo" title="steamworks/FileInfo">FileInfo</a> instead.</div>

</p>
