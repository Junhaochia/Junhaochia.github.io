<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Retrieves a customized list of Steam Workshop addons.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> type</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>The type of items to retrieve. Possible values include:
</p>
<ul><li> popular (All invalid options will equal to this)
</li><li> trending
</li><li> latest
</li><li> friends</div>
</li></ul>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_table" title="Category:table">table</a> tags</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
A table of tags to match.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> offset</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
How much of results to skip from first one. Mainly used for pages.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_4">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> numRetrieve</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
How much items to retrieve, up to 50 at a time.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_5">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> days</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
When getting Most Popular content from Steam, this determines a time period. ( 7 = most popular addons in last 7 days, 1 = most popular addons today, etc )</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_6">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> userID</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
"0" to retrieve all addons, "1" to retrieve addons only published by you, or a valid SteamID64 of a user to get workshop items of.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_7">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_function" title="Category:function">function</a> resultCallback</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The function to process retrieved data. The first and only argument is a table, containing all the info, or nil in case of error</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Retrieves top 10 of Steam Workshop addons.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">steamworks.GetList( &quot;popular&quot;, nil, 0, 10, 7, 0, function( data ) PrintTable( data ) end )</code></pre>
