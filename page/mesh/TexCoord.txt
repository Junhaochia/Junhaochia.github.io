<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the texture coordinates for the next vertex.
</p><p>Non-zero values of stage require the currently bound material to support it. For example, any LightmappedGeneric material supports stages 1 and 2 (lightmap texture coordinates).
</p>
