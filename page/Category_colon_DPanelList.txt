<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelPage">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelPage" title="Form:PanelPage">PanelPage</a></span></span></h1>
<div class="deprecatedfunc"><span>This feature is deprecated.</span><br />
You should avoid using it as it may be removed in a future version.<br /><br />DPanelList was succeeded in version 13 by more specialised layout elements, such as a combination of <a href="/page/Category_colon_DIconLayout" title="Category:DIconLayout">DIconLayout</a>, <a href="/page/Category_colon_DListLayout" title="Category:DListLayout">DListLayout</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_DScrollPanel" title="Category:DScrollPanel">DScrollPanel</a>.</div>
<p>Displays elements in a horizontal or vertical list. A scrollbar is automatically shown if necessary.
Use is not recommended due to deprecation.
</p>
