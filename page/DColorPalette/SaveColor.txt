<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Saves the color of given button across sessions.
</p><p>The color is saved as a panel cookie, see <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetCookie" title="Panel/SetCookie">SetCookie</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetCookieName" title="Panel/SetCookieName">SetCookieName</a>.
</p><p>It is expected that the amount of colors per palette (<a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetCookieName" title="Panel/SetCookieName">SetCookieName</a>) is the same every time.
</p>
