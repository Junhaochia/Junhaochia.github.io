<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets the size of each palette button.
</p><p>This is best kept to such a number, where this equation would return a whole number:<br />
WidthOfColorPalette/ButtonSize=WholeNumber
</p><p>If not, there will be ugly whitespace on the right side of the panel.
