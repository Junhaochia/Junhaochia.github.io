<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Gives you the amount of currently existing entities.
Similar to #<a href="/page/Category_colon_ents" title="Category:ents">ents</a>.<a href="/page/ents/GetAll" title="ents/GetAll">GetAll</a> but with much better performance.
</p>
