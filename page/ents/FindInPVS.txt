<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Finds all entities that lie within a <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://developer.valvesoftware.com/wiki/PVS">PVS</a>.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>

</p>
</td></tr></table>
