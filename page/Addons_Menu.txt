<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="1.Subscribed_addons">1.Subscribed addons </span></h3>
<p>View the addons you are subscribed to, quickly enable or disable all subscriptions. In the list of subscriptions which appears on the right, you can disable/enable and unsubscribe specific addons.
</p><p>When an addon is disabled, it means it is not loaded in the game, but will still be updated when the author makes any changes. When the addon is unsubscribed, it will be removed from the game and will not be available for use, unless you decide to subscribe to the addon again.
</p>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="2._Viewing_addons">2. Viewing addons</span></h3>
<p>Using the set of buttons, you can quickly view addons by categories: Gamemodes, Maps, Weapons, Tools, NPCs and Vehicles.
</p>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="3._Friends_Uploads">3. Friends Uploads</span></h3>
<p>Using the buttons you can view the addons which your friends have uploaded.
</p>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="4._My_Uploads">4. My Uploads</span></h3>
<p>View the addons which yourself has uploaded.
</p>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="5._Open_Workshop">5. Open Workshop</span></h3>
<p>Selecting this option will allow you to open up the Garry's Mod Workshop in the steam browser, the advantage of this is that it offers you with a few search options.
</p>
