<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<div class="internalfunc"><span>This is an internal function or feature.</span><br />
This means you will be able to use it, but you really shouldn't.</div> Fills entStorageTable with all of the entities in a group connected with constraints. Fills constraintStorageTable with all of the constrains constraining the group.
