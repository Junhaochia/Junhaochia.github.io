<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>"Restores the bone's data."<br />
Loops through Bones and calls <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/ManipulateBoneScale" title="Entity/ManipulateBoneScale">ManipulateBoneScale</a>, <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/ManipulateBoneAngles" title="Entity/ManipulateBoneAngles">ManipulateBoneAngles</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/ManipulateBonePosition" title="Entity/ManipulateBonePosition">ManipulateBonePosition</a> on ent with the table keys and the subtable values s, a and p respectively.
</p>
