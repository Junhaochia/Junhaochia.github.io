<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelPage">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelPage" title="Form:PanelPage">PanelPage</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Used to show an icon for any of the <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://steamcommunity.com/stats/GarrysMod/achievements">Garry's Mod Steam achievements</a>.
</p><p>If the viewer has not unlocked the achievement on Steam, the icon will be grayed out.
