<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the internal Lua registry table.
</p><p>The Lua registry is used by the engine and binary modules to create references to Lua values. Avoid creating entries into the registry with a number as the key, as they are reserved for the reference system.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>Improper editing of the registry can result in unintended side effects, including crashing of the game.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
