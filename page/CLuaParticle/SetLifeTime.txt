<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the 'life time' of the particle, how long the particle existed since its creation.
</p><p>This value should always be between 0 and <a href="/page/Category_colon_CLuaParticle" title="Category:CLuaParticle">CLuaParticle</a>:<a href="/page/CLuaParticle/GetDieTime" title="CLuaParticle/GetDieTime">GetDieTime</a>.<br />
It changes automatically as time goes.<br />
If the life time of the particle will be more than <a href="/page/Category_colon_CLuaParticle" title="Category:CLuaParticle">CLuaParticle</a>:<a href="/page/CLuaParticle/GetDieTime" title="CLuaParticle/GetDieTime">GetDieTime</a>, it will be removed.
</p>

<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> lifeTime</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The new life time of the particle.</div>
