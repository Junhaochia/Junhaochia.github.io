<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the current operation of the tool. Does nothing clientside. See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_Tool" title="Category:Tool">Tool</a>:<a href="/page/Tool/SetStage" title="Tool/SetStage">SetStage</a>.
</p><p>Operations and stages work as follows:
</p>
<ul><li> Operation 1
<ul><li> Stage 1
</li><li> Stage 2
</li><li> Stage 3
</li></ul>
</li><li> Operation 2
<ul><li> Stage 1
</li><li> Stage 2
</li><li> Stage ...
</li></ul>
</li></ul>
