<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Initializes the ghost entity with the given model. Removes any old ghost entity if called multiple times.
</p><p>The ghost is a regular prop_physics entity in singleplayer games, and a clientside prop in multiplayer games.
</p>

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The model of the new ghost entity</div>
</div>
