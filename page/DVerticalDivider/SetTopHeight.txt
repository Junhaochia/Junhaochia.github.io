</p><p>The height of the bottom content panel is automatically calculated by taking the total height of the <a href="/page/Category_colon_DVerticalDivider" title="Category:DVerticalDivider">DVerticalDivider</a> and subtracting it with the height of the top content panel and the divider bar.
