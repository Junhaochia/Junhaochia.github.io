<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Displays "swept" box, two boxes connected with lines by their verices.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This function will silently fail if the <b>developer</b> <a href="/page/Category_colon_ConVar" title="Category:ConVar">ConVar</a> is set to 0.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
