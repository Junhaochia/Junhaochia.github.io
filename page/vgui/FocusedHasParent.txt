<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns whether the currently focused panel is a child of the given one.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a> parent</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The parent panel to check the currently focused one against. This doesn't need to be a direct parent (focused panel can be a child of a child and so on).</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_ret"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Returns">Returns</span></h1>
<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_boolean" title="Category:boolean">boolean</a></span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Whether or not the focused panel is a child of the passed one.</div>
</div>
