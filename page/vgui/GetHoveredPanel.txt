<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the panel the cursor is hovering above.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>This returns a cached value that is only updated after rendering and <i>before</i> the next VGUI Think/Layout pass.
</p><p>ie. it lags one frame behind panel layout and is completely unhelpful for <a href="/page/Category_colon_PANEL_Hooks" title="Category:PANEL Hooks">PANEL</a>:<a href="/page/PANEL/Paint" title="PANEL/Paint">Paint</a> if your panels are moving around under the mouse a lot every frame.
</p>
</td></tr></table>

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The panel that the user is currently hovering over with their cursor.</div>
</div>
