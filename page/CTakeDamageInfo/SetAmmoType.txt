<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Changes the ammo type used by the weapon that inflicted the damage.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> ammoType</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Ammo type ID</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates a new DamageInfo object and sets the ammo that caused the damage to AR2 ammo
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local dmginfo = DamageInfo()

dmginfo:SetAmmoType( game.GetAmmoID( 'AR2' ) )</code></pre>
