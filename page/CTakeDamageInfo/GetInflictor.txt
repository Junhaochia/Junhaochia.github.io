<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the inflictor of the damage. This is not necessarily a weapon.
</p><p>For hitscan weapons this is the weapon.<br />
For projectile weapons this is the projectile.
<br /><br />
For a more reliable method of getting the weapon that damaged an entity, use <a href="/page/CTakeDamageInfo/GetAttacker" title="CTakeDamageInfo/GetAttacker">GetAttacker</a> with <a href="/page/Player/GetActiveWeapon" title="Player/GetActiveWeapon">GetActiveWeapon</a>.
