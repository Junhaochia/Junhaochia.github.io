<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Overrides sounds defined inside of a txt file; typically used for adding map-specific sounds.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> filepath</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Path to the script file to load.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Adds and overrides all sounds defined in scripts/override_test.txt
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">sound.AddSoundOverrides(&quot;scripts/override_test.txt&quot;)</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">SoundEmitter: adding map sound overrides from scripts/override_test.txt [1 total, 1 replacements, 0 duplicated replacements]
(Displayed in the console)</div>
