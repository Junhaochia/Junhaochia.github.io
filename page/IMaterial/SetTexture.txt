<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the specified material texture to the specified texture, does nothing on a type mismatch.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> materialTexture</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The name of the keyvalue on the material to store the texture on.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_ITexture" title="Category:ITexture">ITexture</a> texture</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The new texture. This can also be a string, the name of the new texture.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Example usage of this function.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local blur_mat = Material( &quot;pp/bokehblur&quot; )

blur_mat:SetTexture( &quot;$basetexture&quot;, render.GetScreenEffectTexture() )</code></pre>
