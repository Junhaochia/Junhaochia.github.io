<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Enables or disables the clipping used by the VGUI that limits the drawing operations to a panels bounds.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Global/DisableClipping" title="Global/DisableClipping">DisableClipping</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/NoClipping" title="Panel/NoClipping">NoClipping</a>.
