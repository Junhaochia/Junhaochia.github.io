<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the material to be used in all upcoming draw operations using the surface library.
</p><p>Not to be confused with <a href="/page/Category_colon_render" title="Category:render">render</a>.<a href="/page/render/SetMaterial" title="render/SetMaterial">SetMaterial</a>.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_surface" title="Category:surface">surface</a>.<a href="/page/surface/SetTexture" title="surface/SetTexture">SetTexture</a>.
</p>
