<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Loads the specified image from the /cache folder, used in combination <a href="/page/Category_colon_steamworks" title="Category:steamworks">steamworks</a>.<a href="/page/steamworks/Download" title="steamworks/Download">Download</a>.<br />
Most addons will provide a 512x512 png image.
