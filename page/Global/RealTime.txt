<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the uptime of the game/server in seconds (to at least 4 decimal places)
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This is <b>not</b> synchronised or affected by the game.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This will be affected by precision loss if the uptime is more than 30+(?) days, and effectively cease to be functional after 50+(?) days.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<p>You should use this function (or SysTime) for timing real-world events such as user interaction, but not for timing game events such as animations.
</p><p>See also: <a href="/page/Global/CurTime" title="Global/CurTime">CurTime</a>, <a href="/page/Global/SysTime" title="Global/SysTime">SysTime</a>
</p>
