<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Creates or gets the rendertarget with the given name.
</p><p>See <a href="/page/Global/GetRenderTargetEx" title="Global/GetRenderTargetEx">GetRenderTargetEx</a> for an advanced version of this function with more options.
</p>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">

</p>
