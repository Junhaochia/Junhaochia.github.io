<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Adds the given string to the computers clipboard, which can then be pasted in or outside of GMod with Ctrl + V.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> text</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The text to add to the clipboard.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Sets the clipboards text to "Hello!".
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">SetClipboardText( &quot;Hello!&quot; )</code></pre>
