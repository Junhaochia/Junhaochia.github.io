<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Draws the texturize shader, which replaces each pixel on your screen with a different part of the texture depending on its brightness. See <a href="/page/Shaders/g_texturize" title="Shaders/g texturize">g_texturize</a> for information on making the texture.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> Scale</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Scale of the texture. A smaller number creates a larger texture.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> BaseTexture</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
This will be the texture to use in the effect. Make sure you use <a href="/page/Global/Material" title="Global/Material">Material</a> to get the texture number</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Draws the texturize shader with a pattern texture.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function GM:RenderScreenspaceEffects()

 DrawTexturize(1, Material(&quot;pp/texturize/pattern1.png&quot;))

end</code></pre>
