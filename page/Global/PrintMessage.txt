<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Displays a message in the chat, console, or center of screen of every player.
</p><p>This uses the archaic user message system (<a href="/page/Category_colon_umsg" title="Category:umsg">umsg library</a>) and hence is limited to &#8776;250 characters.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> type</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Which type of message should be sent to the players (see <a href="/page/Enums/HUD" title="Enums/HUD">HUD_ Enums</a>)</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> message</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Message to be sent to the players</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Prints into the every player's chat: "I'm new here."
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">PrintMessage(HUD_PRINTTALK, &quot;I'm new here.&quot;)</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">I'm new here.</div>
