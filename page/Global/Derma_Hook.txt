<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Creates panel method that calls the supplied Derma skin hook via <a href="/page/Category_colon_derma" title="Category:derma">derma</a>.<a href="/page/derma/SkinHook" title="derma/SkinHook">SkinHook</a>
