<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Same as <a href="/page/Global/print" title="Global/print">print</a>, except it concatinates the arguments without inserting any whitespace in between them.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Global/Msg" title="Global/Msg">Msg</a>, which doesn't add a newline (<i>"\n"</i>) at the end.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_vararg" title="Category:vararg">vararg</a> args</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
List of values to print. They can be of any type and will be converted to strings with <a href="/page/Global/tostring" title="Global/tostring">tostring</a>.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Prints "Hello, World!" in two lines to the console.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">MsgN(&quot;Hello,&quot;)
MsgN(&quot;World!&quot;)</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<pre class="plain">Hello,
World!</code></pre>
