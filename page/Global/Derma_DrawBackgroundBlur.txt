<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Draws background blur around the given panel.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a> panel</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Panel to draw the background blur around</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> startTime</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Time that the blur began being painted</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Blur being drawn around a panel
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function PANEL:Init()
    self.startTime = SysTime()
end

function PANEL:Paint()
    Derma_DrawBackgroundBlur(self, self.startTime)
end</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Background blur is drawn around the panel</div>
