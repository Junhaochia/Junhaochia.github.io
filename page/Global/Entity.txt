<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the entity with the matching <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/EntIndex" title="Entity/EntIndex">EntIndex</a>.
</p><p>Indices 1 through <a href="/page/Category_colon_game" title="Category:game">game</a>.<a href="/page/game/MaxPlayers" title="game/MaxPlayers">MaxPlayers</a>() are always reserved for players.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>In examples on this wiki, <b>Entity( 1 )</b> is used when a player entity is needed (see <a href="/page/Help:Editing" title="Help:Editing">wiki editing guide</a>). In singleplayer and listen servers, <b>Entity( 1 )</b> will always be the first player. In dedicated servers, however, <b>Entity( 1 )</b> won't always be a valid player.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
