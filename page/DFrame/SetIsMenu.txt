<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets whether the frame is part of a derma menu or not.
</p><p>If this is set to <i>true</i>, <a href="/page/Global/CloseDermaMenus" title="Global/CloseDermaMenus">CloseDermaMenus</a> will not be called when the frame is clicked, and thus any open menus will remain open.
</p>
