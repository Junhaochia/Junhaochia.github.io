<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets the minimum height the DFrame can be resized to by the user.
</p><p>This only applies to users attempting to resize the frame; <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetTall" title="Panel/SetTall">SetTall</a> and similar methods will not be affected. You must call <a href="/page/Category_colon_DFrame" title="Category:DFrame">DFrame</a>:<a href="/page/DFrame/SetSizable" title="DFrame/SetSizable">SetSizable</a> before the user can resize the frame.
</p>
