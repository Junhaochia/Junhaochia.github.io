<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the activity the NPC uses when it moves.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> activity</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The movement activity, see <a href="/page/Enums/ACT" title="Enums/ACT">ACT_ Enums</a>.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Makes all NPCs walk instead of run.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function GM:Think()
	
	for _, npc in pairs(ents.FindByClass(&quot;npc_*&quot;)) do

		if(IsValid(npc) &amp;&amp; npc:IsNPC() &amp;&amp; npc:GetMovementActivity()&#160;!= ACT_WALK) then
			npc:SetMovementActivity(ACT_WALK)
		end

	end
	
end</code></pre>
