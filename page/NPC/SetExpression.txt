<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the NPC's .vcd expression. Similar to <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/PlayScene" title="Entity/PlayScene">PlayScene</a> except the scene is looped until it's interrupted by default NPC behavior or <a href="/page/Category_colon_NPC" title="Category:NPC">NPC</a>:<a href="/page/NPC/ClearExpression" title="NPC/ClearExpression">ClearExpression</a>.
</p>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Function which makes the NPC whom the player is looking at repeat an annoying scene.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function GrenadesScene(ply)

	if(!IsValid(ply)) then return end
	
	local npc = ply:GetEyeTrace().Entity
	
	if(IsValid(npc) &amp;&amp; npc:IsNPC()) then
		npc:SetExpression(&quot;scenes/streetwar/sniper/ba_nag_grenade0&quot;..math.random(1, 5)..&quot;.vcd&quot;)
	end
	
end</code></pre>
