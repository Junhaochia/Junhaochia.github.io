<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Makes the NPC walk toward the given position. The NPC will return to the player after amount of time set by <b>player_squad_autosummon_time</b> <a href="/page/Category_colon_ConVar" title="Category:ConVar">ConVar</a>.
</p><p>Only works on Citizens (npc_citizen) and is a part of the Half-Life 2 squad system.
</p><p>The NPC <b>must</b> be in the player's squad for this to work.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> position</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The target position for the NPC to walk to.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>A console command that makes all Citizens on the map (if they are in the player's squad) try to go to where the player is looking at.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">concommand.Add( &quot;movenpcs&quot;, function( ply )
	for id, npc in pairs( ents.FindByClass( &quot;npc_citizen&quot; ) ) do
		npc:MoveOrder( ply:GetEyeTrace().HitPos )
	end
end )</code></pre>
