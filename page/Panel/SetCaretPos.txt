<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the position of the caret (or text cursor) in a text-based panel object.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> offset</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Caret position/offset from the start of text. A value of <i>0</i> places the caret before the first character.</div>
