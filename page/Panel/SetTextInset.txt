<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the left and top text margins of a text-based panel object, such as a <a href="/page/Category_colon_DButton" title="Category:DButton">DButton</a> or <a href="/page/Category_colon_DLabel" title="Category:DLabel">DLabel</a>.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> insetX</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The left margin for the text, in pixels. This will only affect centered text if the margin is greater than its x-coordinate.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> insetY</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The top margin for the text, in pixels.</div>
</div>
