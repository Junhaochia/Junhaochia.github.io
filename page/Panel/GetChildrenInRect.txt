<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns a table of all visible, selectable children of the panel object that lie at least partially within the specified rectangle.
</p>

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The horizontal (x) position of the top-left corner of the rectangle, relative to the panel object.</div>
</div>
