<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the position of the panel relative to its <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/GetParent" title="Panel/GetParent">GetParent</a>.
</p><p>If you require the panel's position <b>and</b> size, consider using <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/GetBounds" title="Panel/GetBounds">GetBounds</a> instead.<br />
If you need the position in screen space, see <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/LocalToScreen" title="Panel/LocalToScreen">LocalToScreen</a>.
</p>
