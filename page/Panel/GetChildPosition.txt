<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Gets a child object's position relative to this panel object. The number of levels is not relevant; the child may have many parents between itself and the object on which the method is called.
</p>

<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a> pnl</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The panel to get the position of.</div>
</div>
