<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Enables the queue for panel animations. If enabled, the next new animation will begin after all current animations have ended. This must be called before <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/NewAnimation" title="Panel/NewAnimation">NewAnimation</a> to work, and only applies to the next new animation. If you want to queue many, you must call this before each.
</p>
