<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Moves the panel in front of all other panels on screen. Unless the panel has been made a pop-up using <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/MakePopup" title="Panel/MakePopup">MakePopup</a>, it will still draw behind any that have.
</p>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates two frame panels where one acts normal and the other acts as a persistent warning window that will move in front of all other panels until it is closed.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">-- Regular message
local popup1 = vgui.Create(&quot;DFrame&quot;)
popup1:SetSize(400, 300)
popup1:Center()
popup1:MakePopup()
popup1:SetTitle(&quot;This is a normal window.&quot;)

-- Warning message
local popup2 = vgui.Create(&quot;DFrame&quot;)
popup2:SetSize(300, 100)
popup2:Center()
popup2:MakePopup()
popup2:SetTitle(&quot;Warning!&quot;)

-- Warning label
local warning = vgui.Create(&quot;DLabel&quot;, popup2)
warning:SetSize(280, 80)
warning:Center()
warning:SetText(&quot;The server will be shutting down in 5 minutes!&quot;)
warning:SetFont(&quot;GModNotify&quot;)
warning:SetWrap(true)

-- Move the warning message to front constantly
function popup2:Think()
	self:MoveToFront()
end</code></pre>
