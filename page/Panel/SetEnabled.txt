<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the enabled state of a disable-able panel object, such as a <a href="/page/Category_colon_DButton" title="Category:DButton">DButton</a> or <a href="/page/Category_colon_DTextEntry" title="Category:DTextEntry">DTextEntry</a>.

</p>
