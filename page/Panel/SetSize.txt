<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the size of the panel.
</p><p>Calls <a href="/page/Category_colon_PANEL_Hooks" title="Category:PANEL Hooks">PANEL</a>:<a href="/page/PANEL/OnSizeChanged" title="PANEL/OnSizeChanged">OnSizeChanged</a> and marks this panel for layout (<a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/InvalidateLayout" title="Panel/InvalidateLayout">InvalidateLayout</a>).<br />
See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetWidth" title="Panel/SetWidth">SetWidth</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetHeight" title="Panel/SetHeight">SetHeight</a>.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>If you wish to position and re-size panels without much guesswork and have them look good on different screen resolutions, you may find <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/Dock" title="Panel/Dock">Dock</a> useful
</p>
</td></tr></table>
