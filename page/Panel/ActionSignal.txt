<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called whenever a panel receives a command signal from one of its children.
</p><p>This hook is called when using <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/Command" title="Panel/Command">Command</a>, when clicking a <a href="/page/Category_colon_Button" title="Category:Button">Button</a> with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetCommand" title="Panel/SetCommand">SetCommand</a> set, and when clicking text within a <a href="/page/Category_colon_RichText" title="Category:RichText">RichText</a> panel that is marked as click-able.
</p>
