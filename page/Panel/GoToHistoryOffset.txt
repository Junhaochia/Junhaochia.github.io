<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Goes to the page in the HTML panel's history at the specified relative offset.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> offset</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The offset in the panel's back/forward history, relative to the current page, that you would like to skip to. Because this is relative, 0 = current page while negative goes back and positive goes forward. For example, -2 will go back 2 pages in the history.</div>
</div>
