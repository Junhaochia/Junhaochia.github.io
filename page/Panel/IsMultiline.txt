<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Determines whether or not a text-based panel object, such as a <a href="/page/Category_colon_DTextEntry" title="Category:DTextEntry">DTextEntry</a>, is in multi-line mode. This is set with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetMultiline" title="Panel/SetMultiline">SetMultiline</a>.
</p>
