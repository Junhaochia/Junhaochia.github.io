<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Makes this panel droppable. This is used with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/Receiver" title="Panel/Receiver">Receiver</a> to create drag and drop events.
</p><p>Can be called multiple times with different names allowing to be dropped onto different receivers.
</p>

</p>

Name of your droppable panel</div>
</div>
