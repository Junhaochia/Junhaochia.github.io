<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the position and size of the panel.
</p><p>This is equivalent to calling <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/GetPos" title="Panel/GetPos">GetPos</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/GetSize" title="Panel/GetSize">GetSize</a> together.
