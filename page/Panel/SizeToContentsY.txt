<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Resizes the panel object's height to accommodate all child objects/contents.
</p><p>Only works on <a href="/page/Category_colon_Label" title="Category:Label">Label</a> derived panels such as <a href="/page/Category_colon_DLabel" title="Category:DLabel">DLabel</a> by default, and on any panel that manually implemented <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/GetContentSize" title="Panel/GetContentSize">GetContentSize</a> method.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>You must call this function <b>AFTER</b> setting text/font or adjusting child panels.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> addVal=0</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The number of extra pixels to add to the height.</div>
</div>
