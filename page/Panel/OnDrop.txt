<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>We're being dropped on something
We can create a new panel here and return it, so that instead of dropping us - it drops the new panel instead! We remain where we are!
</p><p>Only works for panels derived from <a href="/page/Category_colon_DDragBase" title="Category:DDragBase">DDragBase</a>.
</p>
