<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Resizes the panel object's height so that its bottom is aligned with the top of the passed panel. An offset greater than zero will reduce the panel's height to leave a gap between it and the passed panel.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a> tgtPanel</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The panel to align the bottom of this one with.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> offset=0</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The gap to leave between this and the passed panel. Negative values will cause the panel's height to increase, forming an overlap.</div>
</div>
