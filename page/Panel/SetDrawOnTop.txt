<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Makes the panel render in front of all others, including the spawn menu and main menu.
</p><p>Priority is given based on the last call, so of two panels that call this method, the second will draw in front of the first.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This only makes the panel <b>draw</b> above other panels. If there's another panel that would have otherwise covered it, users will not be able to interact with it.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_boolean" title="Category:boolean">boolean</a> drawOnTop=false</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Whether or not to draw the panel in front of all others.</div>
</div>
