<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Focuses the panel and enables it to receive input.
</p><p>This automatically calls <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetMouseInputEnabled" title="Panel/SetMouseInputEnabled">SetMouseInputEnabled</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetKeyboardInputEnabled" title="Panel/SetKeyboardInputEnabled">SetKeyboardInputEnabled</a> and sets them to true.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Panels derived from <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a> will not work properly with this function. Due to this, any children will not be intractable with keyboard. Derive from <a href="/page/Category_colon_EditablePanel" title="Category:EditablePanel">EditablePanel</a> instead.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
