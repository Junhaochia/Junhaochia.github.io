<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Gets the left and top text margins of a text-based panel object, such as a <a href="/page/Category_colon_DButton" title="Category:DButton">DButton</a> or <a href="/page/Category_colon_DLabel" title="Category:DLabel">DLabel</a>. This is set with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/SetTextInset" title="Panel/SetTextInset">SetTextInset</a>.
</p>
