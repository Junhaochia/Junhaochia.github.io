<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the panels z position which determines the rendering order.
</p><p>Panels with lower z positions appear behind panels with higher z positions.
</p><p>This also controls in which order panels docked with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/Dock" title="Panel/Dock">Dock</a> appears.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> zIndex</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>The z position of the panel.<br />
</p>
Can't be lower than -32768 or higher than 32767.</div>
</div>
