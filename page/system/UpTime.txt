<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the amount of seconds since the Steam user last moved their mouse.
</p><p>This is a direct binding to ISteamUtils-&gt;GetSecondsSinceComputerActive, and is most likely related to Steam's automatic "Away" online status.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This function does not work on Dedicated Servers and will instead return no value.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
