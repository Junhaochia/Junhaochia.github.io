<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Changes the current projected texture between orthographic and perspective projection.
</p><p>You must call <a href="/page/Category_colon_ProjectedTexture" title="Category:ProjectedTexture">ProjectedTexture</a>:<a href="/page/ProjectedTexture/Update" title="ProjectedTexture/Update">Update</a> after using this function for it to take effect.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_boolean" title="Category:boolean">boolean</a> orthographic</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
When false, all other arguments are ignored and the texture is reset to perspective projection.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> left</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The amount of units left from the projected texture's origin to project.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> top</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The amount of units upwards from the projected texture's origin to project.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_4">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> right</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The amount of units right from the projected texture's origin to project.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_5">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> bottom</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The amount of units downwards from the projected texture's origin to project.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Set the projected texture back to perspective projection.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">ProjectedTexture:SetOrthographic( false )
ProjectedTexture:Update()</code></pre>
