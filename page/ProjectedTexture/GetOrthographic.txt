<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the current orthographic settings of the Projected Texture. To set these values, use <a href="/page/Category_colon_ProjectedTexture" title="Category:ProjectedTexture">ProjectedTexture</a>:<a href="/page/ProjectedTexture/SetOrthographic" title="ProjectedTexture/SetOrthographic">SetOrthographic</a>.
