<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the distance at which the projected texture begins its projection.
</p><p>You must call <a href="/page/Category_colon_ProjectedTexture" title="Category:ProjectedTexture">ProjectedTexture</a>:<a href="/page/ProjectedTexture/Update" title="ProjectedTexture/Update">Update</a> after using this function for it to take effect.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Setting this to 0 will disable the projected texture completely! This may be useful if you want to disable a projected texture without actually removing it
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This seems to affect the rendering of shadows - a higher Near Z value will have shadows begin to render closer to their casting object. Comparing a low Near Z value (like 1) with a normal one (12) or high one (1000) is the easiest way to understand this artifact
</p>
</td></tr></table>

<div class="argument">

</p>
