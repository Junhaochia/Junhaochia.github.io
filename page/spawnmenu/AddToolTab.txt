<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Adds a new tool tab to the right side of the spawnmenu via the <a href="/page/Category_colon_SANDBOX_Hooks" title="Category:SANDBOX Hooks">SANDBOX</a>:<a href="/page/SANDBOX/AddToolMenuTabs" title="SANDBOX/AddToolMenuTabs">AddToolMenuTabs</a> hook.
</p><p>This function is a inferior duplicate of <a href="/page/Category_colon_spawnmenu" title="Category:spawnmenu">spawnmenu</a>.<a href="/page/spawnmenu/GetToolMenu" title="spawnmenu/GetToolMenu">GetToolMenu</a>, just without its return value.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> name</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The internal name of the tab. This is used for sorting.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> label=name</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The 'nice' name of the tab (Tip: <a href="/page/Category_colon_language" title="Category:language">language</a>.<a href="/page/language/Add" title="language/Add">Add</a>)</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> icon="icon16/wrench.png"</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The filepath to the icon of the tab. Should be a .png</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates a new tab named, "Tab name!" with a unique name and a wrench icon.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">hook.Add( &quot;AddToolMenuTabs&quot;, &quot;myHookClass&quot;, function()
	spawnmenu.AddToolTab( &quot;Tab name!&quot;, &quot;#Unique_Name&quot;, &quot;icon16/wrench.png&quot; )
end )</code></pre>
