<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Enables the "click when drag-hovering" functionality.
</p><p>If enabled, when hovering over any <a href="/page/Category_colon_DTree_Node" title="Category:DTree Node">DTree_Node</a> of this <a href="/page/Category_colon_DTree" title="Category:DTree">DTree</a> while dragging a panel, the node will be automatically clicked on (and subsequently <a href="/page/Category_colon_DTree" title="Category:DTree">DTree</a>:<a href="/page/DTree/OnNodeSelected" title="DTree/OnNodeSelected">OnNodeSelected</a> will be called) to open any attached panels, such as spawnlists in spawnmenu.
</p><p>See also: <a href="/page/Category_colon_PANEL_Hooks" title="Category:PANEL Hooks">PANEL</a>:<a href="/page/PANEL/DragHoverClick" title="PANEL/DragHoverClick">DragHoverClick</a>.
</p>
