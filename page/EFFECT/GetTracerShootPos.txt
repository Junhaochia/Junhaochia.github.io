<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Used to get the "real" start position of a trace, for weapon tracer effects.
</p><p>"real" meaning in 3rd person, the 3rd person position will be used, in first person the first person position will be used.
</p>

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Default position if we fail</div>
</div>
