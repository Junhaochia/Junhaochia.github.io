<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the player to the chosen team.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> Team</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The team that the player is being set to.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Sets the players team to the first argument when writing "set_team" into the console and respawns the player afterwards, ex. "set_team 1".
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function ChangeMyTeam( ply, cmd, args )
    ply:SetTeam( args[1] )
    ply:Spawn()
end
concommand.Add( &quot;set_team&quot;, ChangeMyTeam )</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Sets the player to team 1 and respawns him/her.</div>
