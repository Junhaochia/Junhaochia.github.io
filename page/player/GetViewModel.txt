<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the player's view model entity by the index.
Each player has 3 view models by default, but only the first one is used.
</p><p>To use the other viewmodels in your SWEP, see <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetWeaponModel" title="Entity/SetWeaponModel">SetWeaponModel</a>.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>In the Client <a href="/page/States" title="States">realm</a>, other players' viewmodels are not available unless they are being spectated.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
