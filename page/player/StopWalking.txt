<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<div class="deprecatedfunc"><span>This feature is deprecated.</span><br />
You should avoid using it as it may be removed in a future version.<br /><br />This appears to be a direct binding to internal functionality that is overridden by the engine every frame so calling these functions may not have any or expected effect.</div>
<p>When used in a <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/SetupMove" title="GM/SetupMove">SetupMove</a> hook, this function behaves unexpectedly by preventing the player from sprinting similar to <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/StopSprinting" title="Player/StopSprinting">StopSprinting</a>.
</p>
