<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Performs a trace hull and applies damage to the entities hit, returns the first entity hit.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>Hitting the victim entity with this function in <a href="/page/Category_colon_ENTITY_Hooks" title="Category:ENTITY Hooks">ENTITY</a>:<a href="/page/ENTITY/OnTakeDamage" title="ENTITY/OnTakeDamage">OnTakeDamage</a> can cause infinite loops.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
