<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns true/false if the player is in specified group or not.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> groupname</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">

</div>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Prints in the players console "yes, I'm awesome!" if he's in the superadmin group.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">if ( Player(2):IsUserGroup(&quot;superadmin&quot;) ) then
    print(&quot;Yes, I'm awesome!&quot;)
end</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">"Yes, I'm awesome!" in console.</div>
