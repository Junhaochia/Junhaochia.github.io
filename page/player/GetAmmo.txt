<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns a table of all ammo the player has.
</p>
<h1 class="first_ret"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Returns">Returns</span></h1>
<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_table" title="Category:table">table</a></span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> Key: AmmoID to be used with functions like <a href="/page/Category_colon_game" title="Category:game">game</a>.<a href="/page/game/GetAmmoName" title="game/GetAmmoName">GetAmmoName</a>.
</p>
<a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> Value: Amount of ammo the player has of this kind.</div>
</div>
