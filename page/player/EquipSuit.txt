<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Equips the player with the HEV suit.
</p><p>Allows the player to zoom, walk slowly, sprint, pickup armor batteries, use the health and armor stations and also shows the HUD.<br />
The player also emits a flatline sound on death, which can be overridden with <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PlayerDeathSound" title="GM/PlayerDeathSound">PlayerDeathSound</a>.
</p><p>The player is automatically equipped with the suit on spawn, if you wish to stop that, use <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/RemoveSuit" title="Player/RemoveSuit">RemoveSuit</a>.
</p>
