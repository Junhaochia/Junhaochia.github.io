<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Displays a message either in their chat, console, or center of the screen. See also <a href="/page/Global/PrintMessage" title="Global/PrintMessage">PrintMessage</a>.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>When called serverside, this uses the archaic user message system (the <a href="/page/Category_colon_umsg" title="Category:umsg">umsg library</a>) and hence is limited to &#8776;250 characters.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p><i>HUD_PRINTCENTER</i> will not work when this is called clientside.
</p>
</td></tr></table>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Prints into the first players chat: "I'm new here."
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">Entity(1):PrintMessage(HUD_PRINTTALK, &quot;I'm new here.&quot;)</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">I'm new here.</div>
