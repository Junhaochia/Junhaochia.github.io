<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the active weapon of the player by its class name.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>This will switch the weapon out of prediction, causing delay on the client and <a href="/page/Category_colon_WEAPON_Hooks" title="Category:WEAPON Hooks">WEAPON</a>:<a href="/page/WEAPON/Deploy" title="WEAPON/Deploy">Deploy</a> to be called out of prediction. Try using <a href="/page/Category_colon_CUserCmd" title="Category:CUserCmd">CUserCmd</a>:<a href="/page/CUserCmd/SelectWeapon" title="CUserCmd/SelectWeapon">SelectWeapon</a> or <a href="/page/Category_colon_input" title="Category:input">input</a>.<a href="/page/input/SelectWeapon" title="input/SelectWeapon">SelectWeapon</a>, instead.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This will trigger the weapon switch event and associated animations. To switch weapons silently, use <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/SetActiveWeapon" title="Player/SetActiveWeapon">SetActiveWeapon</a>.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> className</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The class name of the weapon to switch to.<br /><br />If the player doesn't have the specified weapon, nothing will happen. You can use <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/Give" title="Player/Give">Give</a> to give the weapon first.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Force the player to switch to toolgun
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">Entity(1):SelectWeapon(&quot;gmod_tool&quot;)</code></pre>
<p><br />
</p>

<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example_2">Example</span></h2>

</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local weapons = Entity( 1 ):GetWeapons()
local weapon = weapons[ math.random( #weapons ) ]

Entity( 1 ):SelectWeapon( weapon:GetClass() )</code></pre>
