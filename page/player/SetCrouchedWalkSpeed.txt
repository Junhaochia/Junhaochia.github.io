<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the crouched walk speed multiplier.
</p><p>Doesn't work for values above 1.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/SetWalkSpeed" title="Player/SetWalkSpeed">SetWalkSpeed</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/GetCrouchedWalkSpeed" title="Player/GetCrouchedWalkSpeed">GetCrouchedWalkSpeed</a>.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> speed</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The walk speed multiplier that crouch speed should be.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Set the crouch speed to be as fast as the players walk speed.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">Entity(1):SetCrouchedWalkSpeed( 1 )</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">The player will crouch-walk as fast as normal walking.</div>
