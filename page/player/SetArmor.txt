<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the player armor to the argument.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> Amount</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The amount that the player armor is going to be set to.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Sets the player armor to 100 when he types "GiveArmor" at the console.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">concommand.Add( &quot;GiveArmor&quot;, function( ply )
 ply:SetArmor( 100 )
end )</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Sets the player armor to 100</div>
