<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Disables the default player's crosshair. Can be reenabled with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/CrosshairEnable" title="Player/CrosshairEnable">CrosshairEnable</a>. This will affect <a href="/page/Category_colon_WEAPON_Hooks" title="Category:WEAPON Hooks">WEAPON</a>:<a href="/page/WEAPON/DoDrawCrosshair" title="WEAPON/DoDrawCrosshair">DoDrawCrosshair</a>.
