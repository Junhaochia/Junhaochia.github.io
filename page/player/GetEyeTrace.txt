<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns a table with information of what the player is looking at.
</p><p>The results of this function are cached every frame.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/GetEyeTraceNoCursor" title="Player/GetEyeTraceNoCursor">GetEyeTraceNoCursor</a>
</p>
<h1 class="first_ret"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Returns">Returns</span></h1>
<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_table" title="Category:table">table</a></span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Trace information, see <a href="/page/Structures/TraceResult" title="Structures/TraceResult">TraceResult structure</a></div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Prints the entity the player is looking at.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">print( Entity( 1 ):GetEyeTrace().Entity )</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">"Entity [0][worldspawn]" in console, if you aim at world.</div>
