<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Stops a player from using any inputs, such as moving, turning, or attacking. Key binds are still called. Similar to <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/Freeze" title="Player/Freeze">Freeze</a> but the player takes no damage.
</p><p>Adds the <a href="/page/Enums/FL" title="Enums/FL">FL_FROZEN</a> and <a href="/page/Enums/FL" title="Enums/FL">FL_GODMODE</a> flags to the player.
</p>
