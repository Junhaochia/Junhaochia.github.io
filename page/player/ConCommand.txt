<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Runs the concommand on the player. This does not work on bots.
</p><p>If you wish to directly modify the movement input of bots, use <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/StartCommand" title="GM/StartCommand">StartCommand</a> instead.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Some commands/convars are blocked from being ran/changed using this function, usually to prevent harm/annoyance to clients. For a list of blocked commands, see <a href="/page/Blocked_ConCommands" title="Blocked ConCommands">Blocked ConCommands</a>.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
