<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Slows down the player movement simulation by the timescale, this is used internally in the HL2 weapon stripping sequence.
</p><p>It achieves such behavior by multiplying the <a href="/page/Global/FrameTime" title="Global/FrameTime">FrameTime</a> by the specified timescale at the start of the movement simulation and then restoring it afterwards.
</p>

<td>
<p>This is reset to 1 on spawn
</p>
</td></tr></table>
