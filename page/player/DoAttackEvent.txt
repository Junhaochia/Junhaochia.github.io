<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Starts the player's attack animation. The attack animation is determined by the weapon's HoldType.
</p><p>Similar to other animation event functions, calls <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/DoAnimationEvent" title="GM/DoAnimationEvent">DoAnimationEvent</a> with <a href="/page/Enums/PLAYERANIMEVENT" title="Enums/PLAYERANIMEVENT">PLAYERANIMEVENT_ATTACK_PRIMARY</a> as the event and no extra data.
