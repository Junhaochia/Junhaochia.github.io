<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Freeze the player. Frozen players cannot move, look around, or attack. Key bindings are still called. Similar to <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/Lock" title="Player/Lock">Lock</a> but the player can still take damage.
</p><p>Adds or removes the <a href="/page/Enums/FL" title="Enums/FL">FL_FROZEN</a> flag from the player.
</p>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
<td>
<p>Frozen bots will still be able to look around.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
