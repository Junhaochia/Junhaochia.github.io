<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the angle of the player's view (may rotate body too if angular difference is large)
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Angle" title="Category:Angle">Angle</a> angle</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Angle to set the view to</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Points a player at Vector(0,0,0)
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local ply = LocalPlayer() // Or any player
local vec1 = Vector(0,0,0) -- Where we're looking at
local vec2 = ply:GetShootPos() -- The player's eye pos
ply:SetEyeAngles((vec1 - vec2):Angle()) -- Sets to the angle between the two vectors</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Local player will look at 0,0,0</div>
