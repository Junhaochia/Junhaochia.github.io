<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Inverts the matrix.
</p><p>Inverting the matrix will fail if its <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Determinant">determinant</a> is 0 or close to 0. (ie. its "scale" in any direction is 0.)
</p><p>If the matrix cannot be inverted, it does not get modified.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_VMatrix" title="Category:VMatrix">VMatrix</a>:<a href="/page/VMatrix/InvertTR" title="VMatrix/InvertTR">InvertTR</a>.
</p>
