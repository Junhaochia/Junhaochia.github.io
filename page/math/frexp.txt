<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Used to split the number value into a normalized fraction and an exponent. Two values are returned: the first is a multiplier in the range 1/2 (inclusive) to 1 (exclusive) and the second is an integer exponent.
</p><p>The result is such that x = m*2^e.
</p>
