<p>Addons are files that add new content to your game, such as <a href="/index.php?title=model&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="model (page does not exist)">models</a>, <a href="/index.php?title=vehicle&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="vehicle (page does not exist)">vehicles</a>, <a href="/index.php?title=weapon&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="weapon (page does not exist)">weapons</a>, <a href="/index.php?title=npc&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="npc (page does not exist)">NPCs</a>, or <a href="/page/Using_Tools" title="Using Tools">tools</a> . Addons can be downloaded from the <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/">Garry's Mod Workshop</a> through the Addons menu, located in the <a href="/page/Main_Menu" title="Main Menu">Main Menu</a>.
