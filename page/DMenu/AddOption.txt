<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates a DMenu with 2 options: One that kills yourself; One that does nothing.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local m = DermaMenu()

m:AddOption( &quot;Suicide&quot;, function() RunConsoleCommand(&quot;kill&quot;) end )
m:AddOption( &quot;It does nothing&quot; )

m:Open()</code></pre>
