<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Creates a <a href="/page/Category_colon_DMenuOptionCVar" title="Category:DMenuOptionCVar">DMenuOptionCVar</a> and adds it as an option into the menu. Checking and unchecking the option will alter the given console variable's value.
</p>
