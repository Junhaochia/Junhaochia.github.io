<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the total cost when passing from starting area to the goal area through this node.
</p><p>Used in pathfinding via the <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A*_search_algorithm">A* algorithm</a>.<br />
More information can be found on the <a href="/page/Simple_Pathfinding" title="Simple Pathfinding">Simple Pathfinding</a> page.
</p>
