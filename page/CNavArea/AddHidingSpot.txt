<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Adds a hiding spot onto this nav area.
</p><p>There's a limit of 255 hiding spots per area.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> pos</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The position on the nav area</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> flags=7</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>Flags describing what kind of hiding spot this is.
</p>
<ul><li> 0 = None (Not recommended)
</li><li> 1 = In Cover/basically a hiding spot, in a corner with good hard cover nearby
</li><li> 2 = good sniper spot, had at least one decent sniping corridor
</li><li> 4 = perfect sniper spot, can see either very far, or a large area, or both
</li><li> 8 = exposed, spot in the open, usually on a ledge or cliff
</li></ul>
Values over 255 will be clamped.</div>
</div>
