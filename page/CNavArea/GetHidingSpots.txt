<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns a table of good hiding spots in this area.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_CNavArea" title="Category:CNavArea">CNavArea</a>:<a href="/page/CNavArea/GetExposedSpots" title="CNavArea/GetExposedSpots">GetExposedSpots</a>.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> type=1</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>The type of spots to include.
</p>
<ul><li> 0 = None (Not recommended)
</li><li> 1 = In Cover/basically a hiding spot, in a corner with good hard cover nearby
</li><li> 2 = good sniper spot, had at least one decent sniping corridor
</li><li> 4 = perfect sniper spot, can see either very far, or a large area, or both
</li><li> 8 = exposed, spot in the open, usually on a ledge or cliff, same as GetExposedSpots
</li><li> Values over 255 and below 0 will be clamped.</div>
</li></ul>
</div>
