<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the squared distance of 2 vectors, this is faster than <a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a>:<a href="/page/Vector/Distance" title="Vector/Distance">Distance</a> as calculating the square root is an expensive process.
</p>

</p>

The vector to calculate the distance to.</div>
</div>

</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Checks if a player is within `dist` units of another player in the most efficient way possible.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function PlayerWithinBounds(ply,otherPly, dist)
	return ply:GetPos():DistToSqr(otherPly:GetPos()) &lt; (dist*dist)
	-- This is computationally faster than:
	-- ply:GetPos():Distance(otherPly:GetPos()) &lt; dist
end
print(PlayerWithinBounds(Entity(1),Entity(2),500))</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">true</div>
