<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Translates the <a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> (values ranging from 0 to 1) into a <a href="/page/Structures/Color" title="Structures/Color">Color structure</a>. This will also range the values from 0 - 1 to 0 - 255.
</p><p>x * 255 -&gt; r<br />
y * 255 -&gt; g<br />
z * 255 -&gt; b
</p><p>This is the opposite of <a href="/page/Category_colon_Color" title="Category:Color">Color</a>:<a href="/page/Color/ToVector" title="Color/ToVector">ToVector</a>
</p>
