<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns where on the screen the specified position vector would appear. A related function is <a href="/page/Category_colon_gui" title="Category:gui">gui</a>.<a href="/page/gui/ScreenToVector" title="gui/ScreenToVector">ScreenToVector</a>, which converts a 2D coordinate to a 3D direction.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Should be called from a 3D rendering environment or after <a href="/page/Category_colon_cam" title="Category:cam">cam</a>.<a href="/page/cam/Start3D" title="cam/Start3D">Start3D</a> or it may not work correctly.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
