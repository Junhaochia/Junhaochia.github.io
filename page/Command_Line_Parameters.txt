<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Setting_up_Command_Line_Parameters_in_Steam"> Setting up Command Line Parameters in Steam </span></h1>
<p>To set Command Line Parameters (Launch Options) in Steam for Garry's Mod:
</p>
<ul><li> Open your <b>Steam Library</b> and right click on <b>Garry's Mod</b> and click <b>Properties</b>
</li></ul>
<p><a href="/page/File:Right_Click_GMod.png" class="image"><img alt="Right Click GMod.png" src="/images/6/6f/Right_Click_GMod.png" width="326" height="488" /></a>
</p>
<ul><li> In the pop up window press <b>Set Launch Options...</b>
</li><li> A new pop up window will appear, where you can enter your desired Launch Options, separated by space
</li></ul>
<p><a href="/page/File:Launch_Parameters_GMod.png" class="image"><img alt="Launch Parameters GMod.png" src="/images/3/37/Launch_Parameters_GMod.png" width="616" height="600" /></a>
</p>
<ul><li> After you are done, press OK and then Close and you are done
</li></ul>
