<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Similar to <a href="/page/Category_colon_util" title="Category:util">util</a>.<a href="/page/util/KeyValuesToTable" title="util/KeyValuesToTable">KeyValuesToTable</a> but it also preserves order of keys.
</p>

<div class="argument">

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The key value string</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>

If set to true, will replace \t, \n, \" and \\ in the input text with their escaped variants</div>
</div>
