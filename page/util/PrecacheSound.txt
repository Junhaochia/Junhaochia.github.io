<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Precaches a sound for later use. Sound is cached after being loaded once.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Soundcache is limited to 16384 unique sounds.
