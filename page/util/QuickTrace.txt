<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Performs a trace with the given origin, direction and filter.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> origin</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The origin of the trace.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> endpos</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>The end point of the trace, relative to the start.
</p>
This is the direction of the trace times the distance of the trace.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a> filter=nil</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Entity which should be ignored by the trace. Can also be a table of entities or a function - see <a href="/page/Structures/Trace" title="Structures/Trace">Trace structure</a>.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_ret"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Returns">Returns</span></h1>
<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_table" title="Category:table">table</a></span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Trace result. See <a href="/page/Structures/TraceResult" title="Structures/TraceResult">TraceResult structure</a>.</div>
</div>
