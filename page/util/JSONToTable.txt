<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Converts a JSON string to a Lua table.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>This function converts keys to numbers whenever possible.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
<td>
<p>This will attempt cast the string keys "inf", "nan", "true", and "false" to their respective Lua values. This completely ignores nulls in arrays.
</p><p><b>Issue Tracker: <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://github.com/Facepunch/garrysmod-issues/issues/3561">#3561</a></b>
</p>
</td></tr></table>
