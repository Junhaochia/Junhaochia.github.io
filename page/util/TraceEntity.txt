<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Runs a trace using the entity's collisionmodel between two points. This does not take the entity's angles into account and will trace its unrotated collisionmodel.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Clientside entities will not be hit by traces.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
