</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Double-clicking a file or icon will trigger <b>both</b> this and <a href="/page/Category_colon_DFileBrowser" title="Category:DFileBrowser">DFileBrowser</a>:<a href="/page/DFileBrowser/OnSelect" title="DFileBrowser/OnSelect">OnSelect</a>.

</td></tr></table>

</div>
