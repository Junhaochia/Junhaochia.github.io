<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Performs a trace against this PhysCollide with the given parameters. This can be used for both line traces and box traces.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> origin</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The origin for the PhysCollide during the trace</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Angle" title="Category:Angle">Angle</a> angles</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The angles for the PhysCollide during the trace</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>

</p>

The start position of the trace</div>
</div>

</div>
