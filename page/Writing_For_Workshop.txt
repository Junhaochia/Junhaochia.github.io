<p>When writing code intended for the workshop it is good practice to consider your addons compatability with other workshop addons.<br />
Giving your hooks unique names is an example of this.
</p><p>However sometimes there are many addons available that perform the same task which leads to inevitable overlap.<br />
Collaborators have provided some guidelines to help towards writing such addons.
</p>
