<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the view position and forward angle of a given passenger seat.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> role=0</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The passenger role. 0 is the driver. This parameter seems to be ignored by the game engine and is therefore optional.</div>
</div>
