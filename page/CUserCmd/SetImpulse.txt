<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the impulse command to be sent to the server.
</p><p>For example, 101 is an impulse that will give the player all Half-Life 2 weapons with sv_cheats set to 1. Impulse 100 will toggle their flashlight.
