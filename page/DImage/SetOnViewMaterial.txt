<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Similar to <a href="/page/Category_colon_DImage" title="Category:DImage">DImage</a>:<a href="/page/DImage/SetImage" title="DImage/SetImage">SetImage</a>, but will only do the expensive part of actually loading the textures/material if the material is about to be rendered/viewed.
</p><p>Useful for cases like <a href="/page/Category_colon_DIconBrowser" title="Category:DIconBrowser">DIconBrowser</a>, where there are hundreds of small icons in 1 panel in a list that do not need all to be loaded at the same time.
