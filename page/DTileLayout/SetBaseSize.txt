<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets the size of a single tile. If a child panel is larger than this size, it will occupy several tiles.
</p><p>If you are setting the size of the children properly then you probably don't need to change this.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> size</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The size of each tile. It is recommended you use 2<sup>n</sup> (<i>16, 32, 64...</i>) numbers, and those above <i>4</i>, as numbers lower than this will result in many tiles being processed and therefore slow operation.</div>
</div>
