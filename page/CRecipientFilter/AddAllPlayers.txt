<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Adds all players to the recipient filter.
</p>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Adds all players to a recipient filter, then uses the recipient filter in a usermessage.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function SendMessage()
	local filter = RecipientFilter()
	filter:AddAllPlayers()
	umsg.Start(&quot;message&quot;,filter)
	umsg.End()
end</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Sends a usermessage to every player.</div>
