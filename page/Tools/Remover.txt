<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Purpose"> Purpose </span></h2>
<p>Remove Entities <i>(Props, Vehicles, NPCs, Weapons and so on)</i> with a click of a button while aiming at the object.
</p>
<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Functions"> Functions </span></h2>
<p><b>Left Click: </b>
Remove a single entity.
</p><p><b>Right Click: </b>
Remove entity and all connected entities.
</p><p><b>Reload: </b>
Remove constraints.
</p>
