<p><b>Global States</b> are a Half-Life 2 map-wide system that decides whether a certain gameplay condition is met or not.
</p><p>A Global State can have both, an enabled state and a counter.
</p><p>The list below represents all global states used by Half-Life 2 and its episodes.
</p>
<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Global_States"> Global States </span></h1>

See also <a href="/page/Enums/GLOBAL" title="Enums/GLOBAL">GLOBAL_ Enums</a>.
