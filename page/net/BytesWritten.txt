<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the size of the current message in bytes.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This will include 3 extra bytes used by the engine internally to send the data over the network.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
