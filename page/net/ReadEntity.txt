<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Reads an entity from the received net message. You should always check if the specified entity exists as it may have been removed and therefor NULL if it is outside of the players PVS or was already removed.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>You <b>must</b> read information in same order as you write it.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
