<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets valid spawnpoint classes for use by a team.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>GM.TeamBased must be set to true for this to work
</p>
</td></tr></table>

<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> index</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Index of the team</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
A spawnpoint classname or table of spawnpoint classnames</div>
</div>
