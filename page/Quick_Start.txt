<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Starting_Your_Own_Game">Starting Your Own Game</span></h1>
<p>If you want to run your own <a href="/page/Addons" title="Addons">addons</a>, models or gamemodes, click "Start New Game"
</p><p><a href="/page/File:Tutorial_Start_New_Game.png" class="image"><img alt="Tutorial Start New Game.png" src="/images/0/03/Tutorial_Start_New_Game.png" width="720" height="512" /></a>
</p><p>A menu will appear listing all currently installed maps. Select a map, and click "Start Game".
</p><p><a href="/page/File:Tutorial_Maps_StartGame.png" class="image"><img alt="Tutorial Maps StartGame.png" src="/images/b/b4/Tutorial_Maps_StartGame.png" width="720" height="512" /></a>
</p><p>If you select 'Start Game' without pressing anything you will start on the map gm_flatgrass.
</p>
<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="A_Fresh_Game">A Fresh Game</span></h1>
<p>After the game finished loading you will be standing on your own in an empty map, holding the physgun.
</p><p><a href="/page/File:Tutorial_Spawned.png" class="image" title="View after starting a new game."><img alt="View after starting a new game." src="/images/2/25/Tutorial_Spawned.png" width="720" height="512" /></a>
</p>
<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Start_Playing">Start Playing</span></h1>
<p>A good start would be opening the <a href="/page/Opening_The_Spawnmenu" title="Opening The Spawnmenu">Spawn Menu</a>.
</p><p>To do so, just hold down <span class="keyboard-key" style="display: inline-block; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1);border-radius: 4px;text-align: center; color: #000; padding: 0.2em 0.5em; font-size: 0.85em;">Q</span>.
</p>
<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Miscellaneous">Miscellaneous</span></h1>
<ul><li> If you wish to interact with objects without changing your view angle/perspective, hold down the <span class="keyboard-key" style="display: inline-block; background-color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1);border-radius: 4px;text-align: center; color: #000; padding: 0.2em 0.5em; font-size: 0.85em;">C</span> button. This is called the <a href="/page/The_Context_Menu" title="The Context Menu"> context menu</a>, here you can change your player model, the colour of your player model <b>(Player model colour varies)</b> and change the colour of your Physics Gun.
</li></ul>
