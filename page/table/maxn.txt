<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>

</p>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Demonstrates how this differs from the <b>#</b> operator.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local tbl = {&quot;One&quot;, &quot;Two&quot;, [6] = &quot;Six&quot;, [42] = &quot;Answer to life, the universe, and everything&quot;}

PrintTable(tbl)
print(&quot;\n&quot; .. #tbl)
print(table.maxn(tbl))</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;"><pre><code data-language="lua">1	=	One
2	=	Two
6	=	Six
42	=	Answer to life, the universe, and everything

2
42
</code></pre>
Whereas the length operator (<b>#</b>) returns the highest <i>sequential</i> index, this returns the value of the highest <i>numeric</i> index.</div>
