<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Creates a deep copy and returns that copy.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>This function does NOT copy userdata, such as <a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vectors</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Angle" title="Category:Angle">Angles</a>!
</p>
</td></tr></table>
