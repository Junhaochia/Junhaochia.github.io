<p>Sets whether the <a href="/page/Category_colon_DImageButton" title="Category:DImageButton">DImageButton</a> should keep the aspect ratio of its image. Equivalent of <a href="/page/Category_colon_DImage" title="Category:DImage">DImage</a>:<a href="/page/DImage/SetKeepAspect" title="DImage/SetKeepAspect">SetKeepAspect</a>.
</p><p>Note that this will not try to fit the image inside the button, but instead it will fill the button with the image.
