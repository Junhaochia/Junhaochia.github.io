<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets whether the image inside the <a href="/page/Category_colon_DImageButton" title="Category:DImageButton">DImageButton</a> should be stretched to fill the entire size of the button, without preserving aspect ratio.
</p><p>If set to false, the image will not be resized at all.
