<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Adds a standard notification to your screen.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> text</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The text to display.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> type</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Determines the notification method (e.g. icon) for displaying the notification. See the <a href="/page/Enums/NOTIFY" title="Enums/NOTIFY">NOTIFY_ Enums</a>.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> length</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The number of seconds to display the notification for.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Adds a prop undo notification to the screen, like in Sandbox.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">notification.AddLegacy( &quot;Undone Prop&quot;, NOTIFY_UNDO, 2 )
surface.PlaySound( &quot;buttons/button15.wav&quot; )
Msg( &quot;Prop undone\n&quot; )</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Adds a notice that says "Undone Prop", plays the undo sound, and adds a message to the console.</div>
