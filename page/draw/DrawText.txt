<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Simple draw text at position, but this will expand newlines and tabs.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This is a rendering function that requires a 2D rendering context.<br />This means that it will only work in <a href="/page/Category_colon_2D_Rendering_Hooks" title="Category:2D Rendering Hooks">hooks with a 2D rendering context</a>.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_MarkupObject" title="Category:MarkupObject">MarkupObject</a> for limited width and markup support.
</p>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Makes a message saying "Hello there!" pop up in the center of your screen.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">hook.Add(&quot;HUDPaint&quot;, &quot;HelloThere&quot;, function() 
	draw.DrawText(&quot;Hello there!&quot;, &quot;TargetID&quot;, ScrW() * 0.5, ScrH() * 0.25, Color(255,255,255,255), TEXT_ALIGN_CENTER)
end)</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Hello there!</div>
