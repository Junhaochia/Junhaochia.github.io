<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Gets the REAL weapon table, not a copy. The produced table does <i>not</i> inherit fields from the weapon's base class, unlike <a href="/page/Category_colon_weapons" title="Category:weapons">weapons</a>.<a href="/page/weapons/Get" title="weapons/Get">Get</a>.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>Modifying this table will modify what is stored by the weapons library. Take a copy or use <a href="/page/Category_colon_weapons" title="Category:weapons">weapons</a>.<a href="/page/weapons/Get" title="weapons/Get">Get</a> to avoid this.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
