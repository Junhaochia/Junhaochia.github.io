<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Installing_Addons">Installing Addons</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="To_install_addons">To install addons</span></h4>
<ol><li>Open the <a href="/page/Addons_Menu" title="Addons Menu">Addons Menu</a>.
</li><li>Open one of the catagory tabs (tools, weapons).
</li><li>Find the addon you want.
</li><li>Hover your mouse over the addon and click the install box.
</li></ol>
<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Uninstalling_Addons">Uninstalling Addons</span></h2>
<p>Removes addons from your Addons folder &amp; subscriptions.
</p>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="To_uninstall_addons">To uninstall addons</span></h4>
<ol><li>Open the Addons Menu.
</li><li>Open the Subscriptions tab.
</li><li>Find the addon you want to delete.
</li><li>Hover your mouse over the addon and click the uninstall box.
</li></ol>
<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Enabling_Addons">Enabling Addons</span></h2>
<p>Enables addons so Garry's Mod will load them into the current game.
</p>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="To_enable_addons">To enable addons</span></h4>
<ol><li>Open the Addons Menu.
</li><li>Open the Subscriptions tab.
</li><li>Find the addon you want to enable.
</li><li>Hover your mouse over the addon and click the enable box.
</li></ol>
<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Disabling_Addons">Disabling Addons</span></h2>
<p>Disables addons so Garry's Mod will not load them into the current game.
</p>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="To_disable_addons">To disable addons</span></h4>
<ol><li>Open the Addons Menu.
</li><li>Open the Subscriptions tab.
</li><li>Find the addon you want to disable.
</li><li>Hover your mouse over the addon and click the disable box.
</li></ol>
