<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Creates a keep upright constraint.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This function only works on prop_physics or prop_ragdoll.
</p>
</td></tr></table>

</p>

</div>
