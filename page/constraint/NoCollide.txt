<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Creates an no-collide "constraint". Disables collision between two entities.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Does not work with players.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
