<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets up the scrollbar for use.
</p><p>The scrollbar will automatically disable itself if the total height of the canvas is lower than the height of the panel that holds the canvas during this function call.
</p>

</p>

The size of the panel that holds the canvas, basically size of "1 page".</div>
</div>
