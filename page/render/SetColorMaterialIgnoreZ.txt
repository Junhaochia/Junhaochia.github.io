<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the current drawing material to "color_ignorez".
</p><p>The material is defined as:
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">"UnlitGeneric"
{
	"$basetexture" "color/white"
 	"$model" 		1
	"$translucent" 	1
	"$vertexalpha" 	1
	"$vertexcolor" 	1
	"$ignorez"		1
}
</code></pre>

<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Equivalent of this function
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">render.SetMaterial( Material( &quot;color_ignorez&quot; ) )</code></pre>
