<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Pushes a new clipping plane of the clip plane stack and sets it as active.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>A max of 2 clip planes are supported on Linux/POSIX, and 6 on Windows.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> normal</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The normal of the clipping plane.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> distance</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The distance of the plane from the world origin. You can use <a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a>:<a href="/page/Vector/Dot" title="Vector/Dot">Dot</a> between the normal and any point on the plane to find this, see <a href="#Example">Example 1</a>.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Clips the lower half of your custom entity
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function ENT:Draw()
    local normal = self:GetUp() -- Everything &quot;behind&quot; this normal will be clipped
    local position = normal:Dot( self:GetPos() ) -- self:GetPos() is the origin of the clipping plane

    local oldEC = render.EnableClipping( true )
    render.PushCustomClipPlane( normal, position )

    self:DrawModel()

    render.PopCustomClipPlane()
    render.EnableClipping( oldEC )
end</code></pre>
