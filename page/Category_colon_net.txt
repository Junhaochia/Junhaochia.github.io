<p>The net library is one of a number of ways to send data between the client and server.
</p><p>The major advantages of the net library are the large size limit (64kb/message) and the ability to send data backwards - from the client to the server.
</p><p>Refer to <a href="/page/Net_Library_Usage" title="Net Library Usage">Net Library Usage</a> for a short introduction.
</p>
