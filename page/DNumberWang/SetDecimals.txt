<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>

</p>

<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> num</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The amount of decimal places.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Sets the number selector to 3 decimal places and sets the value to a random number between 0 and 1.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local numinput = vgui.Create(&quot;DNumberWang&quot;)
numinput:SetPos(5, 5)
numinput:SetSize(90, 20)
numinput:SetDecimals(3)

numinput:SetValue(math.random())</code></pre>
