<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets the value of the number selector based on the given fraction number.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> val</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The fraction of the number selector's range.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Sets and prints out the value of a quarter, half, and three-fourths of the number selector range.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local numinput = vgui.Create(&quot;DNumberWang&quot;)
numinput:SetPos(5, 5)
numinput:SetSize(90, 20)

numinput:SetMinMax(0, 500)

numinput:SetFraction(0.25)
print(numinput:GetValue())

numinput:SetFraction(0.5)
print(numinput:GetValue())

numinput:SetFraction(0.75)
print(numinput:GetValue())</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;"><pre><code data-language="lua">125
250
375
</code></pre></div>
