<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the difficulty level of the game.
</p><p><b>TIP:</b> You can use this function in your scripted NPCs or Nextbots to make them harder, however, it is a good idea to lock powerful attacks behind the highest difficulty instead of just increasing the health.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Internally this is tied to the gamerules entity, so you'll have to wait to wait until <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/InitPostEntity" title="GM/InitPostEntity">InitPostEntity</a> is called to return the skill level
</p>
</td></tr></table>
