<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the difficulty level of the game, can be retrieved with <a href="/page/Category_colon_game" title="Category:game">game</a>.<a href="/page/game/GetSkillLevel" title="game/GetSkillLevel">GetSkillLevel</a>.
</p><p>This will automatically change whenever the "skill" convar is modified serverside.
</p>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
<td>
<p>This function will not work if the skill convar doesn't match the targeted value. To work around this, you must use RunConsoleCommand("skill", num) alongside this function.
</p><p><b>Issue Tracker: <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://github.com/Facepunch/garrysmod-issues/issues/3491">#3491</a></b>
</p>
</td></tr></table>
