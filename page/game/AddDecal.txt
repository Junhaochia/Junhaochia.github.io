<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Registers a new decal.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>There's a rather low limit of around 256 for decals that may be registered and they are not cleared on map load.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> decalName</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The name of the decal.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> materialName</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The material to be used for the decal. May also be a list of material names, in which case a random material from that list will be chosen every time the decal is placed.</div>
</div>
