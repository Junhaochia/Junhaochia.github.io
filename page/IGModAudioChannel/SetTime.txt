<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the sound channel to specified time ( Rewind to that position of the song ). Does not work on online radio streams.
</p><p>Streamed sounds must have "noblock" parameter for this to work.
</p>
