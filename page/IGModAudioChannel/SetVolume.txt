<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the volume of a sound channel
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> volume</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Volume to set. 1 meaning 100% volume, 0.5 is 50% and 3 is 300%, etc.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Plays the a Half-Life 1 sound track at 300% volume
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">sound.PlayFile( &quot;sound/music/hl1_song20.mp3&quot;, &quot;&quot;, function( channel )
	if ( IsValid( channel ) ) then
		channel:Play()
		channel:SetVolume( 3 )
	end
end )</code></pre>
