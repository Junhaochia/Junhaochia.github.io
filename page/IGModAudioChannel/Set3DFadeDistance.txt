<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets 3D fade distances of a sound channel.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> min</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>The minimum distance. The channel's volume is at maximum when the listener is within this distance.<br /><br />
</p>
0 or less = leave current.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> max</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>The maximum distance. The channel's volume stops decreasing when the listener is beyond this distance.<br /><br />
</p>
0 or less = leave current.</div>
</div>
