<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>When this flag is set the entity will only transmit to the player when its parent is transmitted. This is useful for things like viewmodel attachments since without this flag they will transmit to everyone (and cause the viewmodels to transmit to everyone too).
</p>
