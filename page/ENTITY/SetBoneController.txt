<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the specified value on the bone controller with the given ID of this entity, it's used in HL1 to change the head rotation of NPCs, turret aiming and so on.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This is the precursor of pose parameters, and only works for Half Life 1: Source models supporting it.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
