<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the entity's local velocity which is their velocity due to movement in the world from forces such as gravity. Does not include velocity from entity-on-entity collision or other world movement.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>Same as <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetAbsVelocity" title="Entity/SetAbsVelocity">SetAbsVelocity</a>, but clamps the given velocity, and is not recommended to be used because of that.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
