<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the entity on fire.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/Extinguish" title="Entity/Extinguish">Extinguish</a>.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> length</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
How long to keep the entity ignited. Not supplying this argument will not ignite the entity at all.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> radius=0</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The radius of the ignition, will ignite everything around the entity that is in this radius.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Ignite all props on the map for 30 seconds.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">for k, v in pairs(ents.FindByClass(&quot;prop_physics&quot;)) do
   v:Ignite(30)
end</code></pre>
