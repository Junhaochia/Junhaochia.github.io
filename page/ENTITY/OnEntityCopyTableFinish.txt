<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called after duplicator finishes saving the entity, allowing you to modify the save data.
</p><p>This is called after <a href="/page/Category_colon_ENTITY_Hooks" title="Category:ENTITY Hooks">ENTITY</a>:<a href="/page/ENTITY/PostEntityCopy" title="ENTITY/PostEntityCopy">PostEntityCopy</a>.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_table" title="Category:table">table</a> data</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The save <a href="/page/Structures/EntityCopyData" title="Structures/EntityCopyData">EntityCopyData structure</a> that you can modify.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Prevent this entity from being copied and subsequently pasted.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function ENT:OnEntityCopyTableFinish( data )
	for k, v in pairs( data ) do data[ k ] = nil end
end</code></pre>
