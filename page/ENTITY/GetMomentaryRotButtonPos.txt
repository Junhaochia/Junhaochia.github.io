<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the amount a momentary_rot_button entity is turned based on the given angle. 0 meaning completely turned closed, 1 meaning completely turned open.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>This only works on momentary_rot_button entities.
</p>
</td></tr></table>

<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Angle" title="Category:Angle">Angle</a> turnAngle</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The angle of rotation to compare - usually should be <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/GetAngles" title="Entity/GetAngles">GetAngles</a>.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_ret"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Returns">Returns</span></h1>
<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a></span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The amount the momentary_rot_button is turned, ranging from 0 to 1, or nil if the entity is not a momentary_rot_button.</div>
