<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets custom bone scale.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>When used serverside, this method produces a huge network consumption!
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
<td>
<p>This does not scale procedural bones.
</p><p><b>Issue Tracker: <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://github.com/Facepunch/garrysmod-issues/issues/3502">#3502</a></b>
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> boneID</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Index of the bone you want to manipulate</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
