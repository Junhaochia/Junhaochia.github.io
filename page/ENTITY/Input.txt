<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Fires input to the entity with the ability to make another entity responsible.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/Fire" title="Entity/Fire">Fire</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/AcceptInput" title="GM/AcceptInput">AcceptInput</a>.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> input</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The name of the input to fire</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a> activator</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The entity that caused this input (EG the player who pushed a button)</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>

</div>
