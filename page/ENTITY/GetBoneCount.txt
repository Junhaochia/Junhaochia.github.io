<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the amount of bones in the entity.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Will return -1 for <a href="/page/Global/ClientsideModel" title="Global/ClientsideModel">ClientsideModel</a> or undrawn entities until <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetupBones" title="Entity/SetupBones">SetupBones</a> is called on the entity.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_ret"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Returns">Returns</span></h1>
<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a></span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The amount of bones in given entity; -1 on failure.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Prints amount of bones in player 1
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">print( Entity(1):GetBoneCount() )</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">The amount of bones in player 1, which normally would be 68.</div>
