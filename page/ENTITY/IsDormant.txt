<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns whether the entity is dormant or not. Client/server entities become dormant when they leave the PVS on the server. Client side entities can decide for themselves whether to become dormant. This mainly applies to PVS.
</p>
