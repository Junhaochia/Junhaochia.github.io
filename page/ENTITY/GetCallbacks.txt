<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the specified hook callbacks for this entity added with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/AddCallback" title="Entity/AddCallback">AddCallback</a>

</p>
