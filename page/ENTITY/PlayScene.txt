<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Makes the entity play a .vcd scene.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> scene</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Filepath to scene</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> delay=0</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Delay in seconds until the scene starts playing.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Makes the entity that the player 1 is looking at play the "Welcome to City 17" speech.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local ent = Entity( 1 ):GetEyeTrace().Entity

if( IsValid( ent ) ) then
	npc:PlayScene( &quot;scenes/breencast/welcome.vcd&quot; )
end</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">The entity plays the scene.</div>
