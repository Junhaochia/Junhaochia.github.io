<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the entity's velocity. For entities with physics, consider using <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a>:<a href="/page/PhysObj/SetVelocity" title="PhysObj/SetVelocity">SetVelocity</a> on the <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a> of the entity.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Actually binds to CBaseEntity::SetBaseVelocity() which sets the entity's velocity due to forces applied by other entities.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>If applied to a player, this will actually <b>ADD</b> velocity, not set it.
</p>
</td></tr></table>

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The new velocity to set.</div>
