<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Forces the entity to reconfigure its bones. You might need to call this after changing your model's scales or when manually drawing the entity multiple times at different positions.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This calls the BuildBonePositions callback added via <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/AddCallback" title="Entity/AddCallback">AddCallback</a>, so avoid calling this function inside it to prevent an infinite loop.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
