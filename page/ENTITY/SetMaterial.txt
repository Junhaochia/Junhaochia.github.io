<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the rendering material override of the entity.
</p><p>To set a Lua material created with <a href="/page/Global/CreateMaterial" title="Global/CreateMaterial">CreateMaterial</a>, just prepend a "!" to the material name.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>If you wish to override a single material on the model, use <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetSubMaterial" title="Entity/SetSubMaterial">SetSubMaterial</a> instead.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Please note that to apply materials to models, that material <b>must</b> have VertexLitGeneric shader. For that reason you cannot apply map textures onto models, map textures use a different material shader - LightmappedGeneric.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
