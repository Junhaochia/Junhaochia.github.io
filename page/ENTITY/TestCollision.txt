<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Allows you to override trace result when a trace hits the entitys Bounding Box.<br /><br />
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Your entity must have <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/EnableCustomCollisions" title="Entity/EnableCustomCollisions">EnableCustomCollisions</a> enabled for this hook to work
</p>
</td></tr></table>
