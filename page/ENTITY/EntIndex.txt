<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Gets the unique entity index of an entity.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Entity indices are marked as unused after deletion, and can be reused by newly-created entities
</p>
</td></tr></table>

<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a></span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>The index of the entity.<br />
</p>
<br />-1 for clientside-only or serverside-only entities.</div>
</div>
