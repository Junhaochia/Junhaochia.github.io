<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the matrix (position / rotation transform) of a given bone entity.
</p>

<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
<td>

</p>

<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
<td>

</p>
