<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Manipulates the bone's jiggle status. This allows non jiggly bones to become jiggly.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> boneID</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Index of the bone you want to manipulate.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> enabled</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>0 = No Jiggle
</p>
1 = Jiggle</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Turn everyone into jelly mode
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">for k, v in pairs(player.GetAll()) do
	local i = 0

	while i &lt; v:GetBoneCount() do
		v:ManipulateBoneJiggle(i, 1)
		i = i + 1
	end
end</code></pre>
