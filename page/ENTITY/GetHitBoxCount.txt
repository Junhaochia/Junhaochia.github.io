<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Gets how many hit boxes are in a given hit box group
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> group</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The number of the hit box group</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_ret"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Returns">Returns</span></h1>
<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a></span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The number of hit boxes.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Will print out how many hit boxes the client has in each of their hit box groups
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local numHitBoxGroups = LocalPlayer():GetHitBoxGroupCount()

for i=0, numHitBoxGroups - 1 do
  local numHitBoxes = LocalPlayer():GetHitBoxCount( i )
  print(&quot;Hit box group &quot; .. i .. &quot; has &quot; .. numHitBoxes .. &quot; hit boxes!&quot;)
end</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">"Hit box group 0 has 17 hit boxes!"</div>
