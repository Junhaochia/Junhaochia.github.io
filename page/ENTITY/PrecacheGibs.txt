<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Precaches gibs for the entity's model.
</p><p>Normally this function should be ran when the entity is spawned, for example the <a href="/page/Category_colon_ENTITY_Hooks" title="Category:ENTITY Hooks">ENTITY</a>:<a href="/page/ENTITY/Initialize" title="ENTITY/Initialize">Initialize</a>, after <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetModel" title="Entity/SetModel">SetModel</a> is called.
</p><p>This is required for <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/GibBreakServer" title="Entity/GibBreakServer">GibBreakServer</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/GibBreakClient" title="Entity/GibBreakClient">GibBreakClient</a> to work.
</p>
