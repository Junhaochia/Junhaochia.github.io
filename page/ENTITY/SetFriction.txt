<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets how much friction an entity has when sliding against a surface. Entities default to 1 (100%) and can be higher or even negative.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Works only for MOVETYPE_STEP entities.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
