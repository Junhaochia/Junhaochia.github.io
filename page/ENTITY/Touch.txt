<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called every tick for every entity being "touched".
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_ENTITY_Hooks" title="Category:ENTITY Hooks">ENTITY</a>:<a href="/page/ENTITY/StartTouch" title="ENTITY/StartTouch">StartTouch</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_ENTITY_Hooks" title="Category:ENTITY Hooks">ENTITY</a>:<a href="/page/ENTITY/EndTouch" title="ENTITY/EndTouch">EndTouch</a>.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>This only works for <b>brush</b> entities and for entities that have <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetTrigger" title="Entity/SetTrigger">SetTrigger</a> set to true.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>For physics enabled entities, this hook will <b>not</b> be ran while the entity's physics is asleep. See <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a>:<a href="/page/PhysObj/Wake" title="PhysObj/Wake">Wake</a>.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
