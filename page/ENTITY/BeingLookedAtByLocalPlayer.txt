<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns true if the entity is being looked at by the local player and is within 256 units of distance.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This function is only available in entities that are based off of sandbox's base_gmodentity
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This function uses Distance instead of DistToSqr so it may not be suitable in situations where it's called often
</p>
</td></tr></table>
