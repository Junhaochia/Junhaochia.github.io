<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the render bounds for the entity in world space coordinates. For relative coordinates see <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetRenderBounds" title="Entity/SetRenderBounds">SetRenderBounds</a>.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> mins</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The minimum corner of the bounds, relative to origin of the world/map.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The maximum corner of the bounds, relative to origin of the world/map.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> add=Vector( 0, 0, 0 )</span>
</p>
