<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns a centered vector of this entity, NPCs use this internally to aim at their targets.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This only works on players and NPCs.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
