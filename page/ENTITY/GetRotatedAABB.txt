<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>

</p>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>The entity's AABB center (regardless of model origin) is stored in 'pos'.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local a, b = ent:GetRotatedAABB( v:OBBMins(), v:OBBMaxs() )
local pos = ( ent:GetPos() + ( a + b ) / 2 )</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">pos has the coordinates of the AABB center.</div>
