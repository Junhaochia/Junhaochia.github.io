<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the entity's model bounds. This is different than the collision bounds/hull. This is not scaled with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetModelScale" title="Entity/SetModelScale">SetModelScale</a>, and will return the model's original, unmodified mins and maxs.
</p>
