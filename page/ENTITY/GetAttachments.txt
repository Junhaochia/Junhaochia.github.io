<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns a table containing all attachments of the given entitys model.<br />
Returns an empty table or <b>nil</b> in case it's model has no attachments.
</p>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
