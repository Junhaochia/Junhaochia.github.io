<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Advances the cycle of an animated entity.
</p><p>Animations that loop will automatically reset the cycle so you don't have to - ones that do not will stop animating once you reach the end of their sequence.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> interval</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
How many seconds to advance the cycle. Set to 0 to let the engine calculate this for you based on <a href="/page/Global/CurTime" title="Global/CurTime">CurTime</a>.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>From DModelPanel
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function PANEL:RunAnimation()
	self.Entity:FrameAdvance( (RealTime()-self.LastPaint) * self.m_fAnimSpeed )	
end</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Animates the entity</div>
