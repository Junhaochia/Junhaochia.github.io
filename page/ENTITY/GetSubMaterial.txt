<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the material override for the given index.
</p><p>Returns "" if no material override exists. Use <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/GetMaterials" title="Entity/GetMaterials">GetMaterials</a> to list it's default materials.
</p>
