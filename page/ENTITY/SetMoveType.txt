<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the entity's move type. This should be called before initializing the physics object on the entity, unless it will override SetMoveType such as <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/PhysicsInitBox" title="Entity/PhysicsInitBox">PhysicsInitBox</a>.
</p><p>Despite existing on client, it doesn't actually do anything on client.
