<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns whether or not the bone manipulation functions have ever been called on given entity.
</p><p>Related functions are <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/ManipulateBonePosition" title="Entity/ManipulateBonePosition">ManipulateBonePosition</a>, <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/ManipulateBoneAngles" title="Entity/ManipulateBoneAngles">ManipulateBoneAngles</a>, <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/ManipulateBoneJiggle" title="Entity/ManipulateBoneJiggle">ManipulateBoneJiggle</a>, and <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/ManipulateBoneScale" title="Entity/ManipulateBoneScale">ManipulateBoneScale</a>.
</p>
