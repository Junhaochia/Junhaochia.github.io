<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns an entity's collision bounding box. In most cases, this will return the same bounding box as <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/GetModelBounds" title="Entity/GetModelBounds">GetModelBounds</a> unless the entity does not have a physics mesh or it has a <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a> different from the default.
</p>
