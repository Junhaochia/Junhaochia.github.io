<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns an integer that represents how deep in water the entity is.
</p><p><br />
</p>
<ul><li> <b>0</b> - The entity isn't in water.
</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>1</b> - Slightly submerged (at least to the feet).
</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>2</b> - The majority of the entity is submerged (at least to the waist).
</li></ul>
<ul><li> <b>3</b> - Completely submerged.
</li></ul>
