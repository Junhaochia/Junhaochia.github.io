<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Restarts the entity's animation gesture. If the given gesture is already playing, it will reset it and play it from the beginning.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This function only works on <a href="/page/BaseAnimatingOverlay" title="BaseAnimatingOverlay">BaseAnimatingOverlay</a> entites.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
