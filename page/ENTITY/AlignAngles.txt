<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns an angle based on the ones inputted that you can use to align an object.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This function doesn't change the angle of the entity on its own (see example).
</p>
</td></tr></table>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>This example will make ent1 face up from ent2.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">ent1:SetAngles(ent1:AlignAngles(ent1:GetForward():Angle(), ent2:GetUp():Angle()))</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Sets ent1's angle to one where ent1 faces up from ent2.</div>
