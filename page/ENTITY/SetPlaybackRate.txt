<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Allows you to set how fast an entity's animation will play, with 1.0 being the default speed.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> fSpeed</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
How fast the animation will play.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Makes Entity(1)'s viewmodel play animations 50% slower.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">Entity(1):GetViewModel():SetPlaybackRate(0.5)</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Entity(1)'s viewmodel now plays animations 50% slower.</div>
