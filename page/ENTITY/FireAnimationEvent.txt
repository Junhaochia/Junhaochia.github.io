<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called before firing clientside animation events, such as muzzle flashes or shell ejections.
</p><p>See <a href="/page/Category_colon_ENTITY_Hooks" title="Category:ENTITY Hooks">ENTITY</a>:<a href="/page/ENTITY/HandleAnimEvent" title="ENTITY/HandleAnimEvent">HandleAnimEvent</a> for the serverside version.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This hook only works on "anim", "nextbot" and "ai" type entities.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
