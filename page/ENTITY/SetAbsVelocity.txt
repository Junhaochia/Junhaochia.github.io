<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the entity's velocity.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Actually binds to CBaseEntity::SetLocalVelocity() which sets the entity's velocity due to movement in the world from forces such as gravity. Does not include velocity from entity-on-entity collision or other world movement.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Vector" title="Category:Vector">Vector</a> velocity</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The new velocity to set.</div>
</div>
