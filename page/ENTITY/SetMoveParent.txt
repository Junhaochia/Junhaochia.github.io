<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Sets the Movement Parent of an entity to another entity.
</p><p>Similar to <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/SetParent" title="Entity/SetParent">SetParent</a>, except the object's coordinates are not translated automatically before parenting.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a> Parent</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The entity to change this entity's Movement Parent to.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Sets the Movement Entity of a new entity to Player 1.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local hat = ents.Create(&quot;prop_physics&quot;)
//Position and angles are relative to our future parent.
hat:SetPos(Vector(0,0,10))
hat:SetAngles(Angle(0,90,0))

hat:SetMoveParent(Entity(1))

hat:Spawn()</code></pre>
