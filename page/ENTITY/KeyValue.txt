<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when the engine sets a value for this scripted entity.
</p><p>See <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/EntityKeyValue" title="GM/EntityKeyValue">EntityKeyValue</a> for a hook that works for all entities.<br />

</p>
