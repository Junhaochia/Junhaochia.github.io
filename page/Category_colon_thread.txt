<p>Threads in Lua are returned by the <a href="/page/Category_colon_coroutine" title="Category:coroutine">coroutine</a>.<a href="/page/coroutine/create" title="coroutine/create">create</a> function. They have no properties or member functions, and are used by the <a href="/page/Category_colon_coroutine" title="Category:coroutine">coroutine</a>.<a href="/page/coroutine/resume" title="coroutine/resume">resume</a> function.
</p><p>Be aware that coroutines are not multi-threading in the traditional sense. Coroutines are not run in parallel. See <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://lua-users.org/wiki/CoroutinesTutorial">here</a> for more information on Lua coroutines.
</p>
