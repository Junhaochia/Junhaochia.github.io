<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Gets the address of a function from a list of 20 functions, for the list see <a href="/page/Ircalladdr_Functions" title="Ircalladdr Functions">Ircalladdr Functions</a>
</p>
