<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Returns the angles of the model viewing camera. Is <b>nil</b> until changed with <a href="/page/Category_colon_DModelPanel" title="Category:DModelPanel">DModelPanel</a>:<a href="/page/DModelPanel/SetLookAng" title="DModelPanel/SetLookAng">SetLookAng</a>.
</p>

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The angles of the camera.</div>
</div>

</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local mdl = vgui.Create(&quot;DModelPanel&quot;)
mdl:SetPos(20, 20)
mdl:SetSize(200, 200)
mdl:SetModel(&quot;models/props_junk/watermelon01.mdl&quot;)

print(mdl:GetLookAng())</code></pre>
