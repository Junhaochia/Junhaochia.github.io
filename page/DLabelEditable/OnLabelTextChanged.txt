<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelHook">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelHook" title="Form:PanelHook">PanelHook</a></span></span></h1>
<p>A hook called when the player presses Enter (i.e. the finished editing the label) and the text has changed.
</p><p>Allows you to override/modify the text that will be set to display.
