<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called whenever a players releases a mouse key on the context menu in Sandbox or on any panel derived from CGModBase, such as the panel used by <a href="/page/Category_colon_gui" title="Category:gui">gui</a>.<a href="/page/gui/EnableScreenClicker" title="gui/EnableScreenClicker">EnableScreenClicker</a> and the panel used by <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/ParentToHUD" title="Panel/ParentToHUD">ParentToHUD</a>.
</p>
