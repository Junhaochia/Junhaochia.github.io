<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when a player has been hit by a trace and damaged (such as from a bullet). Returning true overrides the damage handling and prevents <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/ScalePlayerDamage" title="GM/ScalePlayerDamage">ScalePlayerDamage</a> from being called.
</p>
