<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Makes your forward/radial blur pulse.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local function GetNewMotionBlurValues( h, v, f, r )
    f = f * math.sin( CurTime() * 5 )
    return h, v, f, r
end
hook.Add( &quot;GetMotionBlurValues&quot;, &quot;GetNewMotionBlurValues&quot;, GetNewMotionBlurValues )</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Your radial blur pulses.</div>
