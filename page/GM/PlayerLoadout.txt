<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called to give players the default set of weapons.<br />
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This function may not work in your custom gamemode if you have overridden your <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PlayerSpawn" title="GM/PlayerSpawn">PlayerSpawn</a> and you do not use self.BaseClass.PlayerSpawn or <a href="/page/Category_colon_hook" title="Category:hook">hook</a>.<a href="/page/hook/Call" title="hook/Call">Call</a>.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a> ply</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Player to give weapons to.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Gives the player only a pistol.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function GM:PlayerLoadout( ply )
	ply:Give( &quot;weapon_pistol&quot; )

	-- Prevent default Loadout.
	return true
end</code></pre>
