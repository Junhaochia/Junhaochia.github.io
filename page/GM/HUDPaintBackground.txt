<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called before <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/HUDPaint" title="GM/HUDPaint">HUDPaint</a> when the HUD background is being drawn.<br />
Things rendered in this hook will <b>always</b> appear behind things rendered in <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/HUDPaint" title="GM/HUDPaint">HUDPaint</a>.
</p>

</p>
