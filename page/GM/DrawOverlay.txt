<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called after all other 2D draw hooks are called. Draws over all VGUI Panels and HUDs.
</p><p>Unlike <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/HUDPaint" title="GM/HUDPaint">HUDPaint</a>, this hook is called with the game paused and while the Camera SWEP is equipped.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Only be called when r_drawvgui is enabled
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This is a rendering hook with a 2D rendering context.<br />This means that the only rendering functions will work in it are <a href="/page/Category_colon_2D_Rendering_Functions" title="Category:2D Rendering Functions">functions with a 2D rendering context</a>.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
