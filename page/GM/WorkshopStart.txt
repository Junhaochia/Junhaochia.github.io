<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when downloading content from Steam workshop begins. Used by default to show fancy workshop downloading panel.
</p><p>The order of Workshop hooks is this:
</p>
<ul><li> WorkshopStart
</li><li> WorkshopDownloadTotals
<ul><li> These are called for each new item:
<ul><li> WorkshopDownloadFile
</li><li> WorkshopDownloadProgress - This is called until the file is finished
</li><li> WorkshopDownloadedFile
</li></ul>
</li></ul>
</li><li> WorkshopEnd
</li></ul>
