<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called every time a bullet is fired from an entity.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>This hook is called directly from <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/FireBullets" title="Entity/FireBullets">FireBullets</a>. Due to this, you cannot call <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/FireBullets" title="Entity/FireBullets">FireBullets</a> inside this hook or an infinite loop will occur crashing the game.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
