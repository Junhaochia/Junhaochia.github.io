<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when an NPC takes damage.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This hook is called only when a specific hit group of the NPC is hit. In cases where the hitgroup doesn't matter, you should use <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/EntityTakeDamage" title="GM/EntityTakeDamage">EntityTakeDamage</a> instead!
</p>
</td></tr></table>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Double the damage whenever an NPC is hurt.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">hook.Add( &quot;ScaleNPCDamage&quot;, &quot;ScaleNPCDamageExample&quot;, function( npc, hitgroup, dmginfo )
	dmginfo:ScaleDamage( 2 )
end )</code></pre>
