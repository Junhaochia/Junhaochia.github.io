<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Handles the player's death.
</p><p>This hook is <b>not</b> called if the player is killed by <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/KillSilent" title="Player/KillSilent">KillSilent</a>. See <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PlayerSilentDeath" title="GM/PlayerSilentDeath">PlayerSilentDeath</a> for that.
</p>
<ul><li> <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PlayerDeath" title="GM/PlayerDeath">PlayerDeath</a> is called after this hook
</li><li> <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PostPlayerDeath" title="GM/PostPlayerDeath">PostPlayerDeath</a> is called after that
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
