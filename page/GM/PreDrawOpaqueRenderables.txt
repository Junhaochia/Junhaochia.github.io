<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called before all opaque entities are drawn.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PreDrawTranslucentRenderables" title="GM/PreDrawTranslucentRenderables">PreDrawTranslucentRenderables</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PostDrawOpaqueRenderables" title="GM/PostDrawOpaqueRenderables">PostDrawOpaqueRenderables</a>.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This is a rendering hook with a 3D rendering context.<br />This means that the only rendering functions will work in it are <a href="/page/Category_colon_3D_Rendering_Functions" title="Category:3D Rendering Functions">functions with a 3D rendering context</a>.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
