<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when a player switches their weapon.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This hook is <a href="/page/Prediction" title="Prediction">predicted</a>. This means that in singleplayer, it will <i>not</i> be called in the Client realm.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a> player</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The player switching weapons.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_2">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Weapon" title="Category:Weapon">Weapon</a> oldWeapon</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The previous weapon. Will be NULL if the previous weapon was removed or the player is switching from nothing.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments_3">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_Weapon" title="Category:Weapon">Weapon</a> newWeapon</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
<p>The weapon the player switched to. Will be NULL if the player is switching to nothing.
</p>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
<td>
<p>This can be NULL on the client if the weapon hasn't been created over the network yet.
</p><p><b>Issue Tracker: <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://github.com/Facepunch/garrysmod-issues/issues/2922">#2922</a></b>
</p>
</td></tr></table></div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_ret"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Returns">Returns</span></h1>
<div class="return" style="counter-increment: returns;">
<p><span class="ret_number" style="counter-increment: returns -1;"></span> <span class="ret_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_boolean" title="Category:boolean">boolean</a></span>
</p>

Return true to prevent weapon switch</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>The players weapon information will be printed when the player switched weapons.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function GM:PlayerSwitchWeapon(ply, oldWeapon, newWeapon)
	//GetClass() will return the weapons class as a string.
	MsgN(&quot;You switched weapons! Your old weapon is &quot; .. oldWeapon:GetClass() ..&quot;.&quot;)
	MsgN(&quot;Your new weapon is &quot; .. newWeapon:GetClass() .. &quot;.&quot;);
end</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<pre class="plain">You switched weapons! Your old weapon is gmod_camera.
Your new weapon is weapon_crossbow.</code></pre>
