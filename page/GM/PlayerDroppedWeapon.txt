<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when a weapon is dropped by a player via <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/DropWeapon" title="Player/DropWeapon">DropWeapon</a>.
</p><p>See also <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/WeaponEquip" title="GM/WeaponEquip">WeaponEquip</a> for a hook when a player picks up a weapon.
</p><p>The weapon's <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/GetOwner" title="Entity/GetOwner">GetOwner</a> will be NULL at the time this hook is called.<br />
<a href="/page/Category_colon_WEAPON_Hooks" title="Category:WEAPON Hooks">WEAPON</a>:<a href="/page/WEAPON/OnDrop" title="WEAPON/OnDrop">OnDrop</a> will be called before this hook is.
</p>
