<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when the mouse has been double clicked on any panel derived from CGModBase, such as the panel used by <a href="/page/Category_colon_gui" title="Category:gui">gui</a>.<a href="/page/gui/EnableScreenClicker" title="gui/EnableScreenClicker">EnableScreenClicker</a> and the panel used by <a href="/page/Category_colon_Panel" title="Category:Panel">Panel</a>:<a href="/page/Panel/ParentToHUD" title="Panel/ParentToHUD">ParentToHUD</a>.
</p><p>By default this hook calls <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/GUIMousePressed" title="GM/GUIMousePressed">GUIMousePressed</a>.
</p>
