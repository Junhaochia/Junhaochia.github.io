<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called before any of 2D drawing functions. Drawing anything in it seems to work incorrectly.
</p>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Allows you to draw something before any other HUD elements.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local clr = Color(20, 20, 20, 200)
hook.Add(&quot;PreDrawHUD&quot;, &quot;PreDrawExample&quot;, function()
	cam.Start2D() -- If you don't call this the drawing will not work properly.
	
	surface.SetDrawColor(clr)
	surface.DrawRect(0, 0, ScrW(), ScrH())
	
	cam.End2D()
end)</code></pre>
