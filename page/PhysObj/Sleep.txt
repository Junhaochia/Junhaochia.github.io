<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Makes the physics object "sleep".
</p><p>The physics object will no longer be moving unless it is "woken up" by either a collision with another moving object, or by <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a>:<a href="/page/PhysObj/Wake" title="PhysObj/Wake">Wake</a>. This is an optimization feature of the physics engine.
</p>
