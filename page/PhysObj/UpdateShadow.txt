<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Unlike <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a>:<a href="/page/PhysObj/SetPos" title="PhysObj/SetPos">SetPos</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a>:<a href="/page/PhysObj/SetAngles" title="PhysObj/SetAngles">SetAngles</a>, this allows the movement of a physobj while leaving physics interactions intact.<br />
This is used internally by the motion controller of the Gravity Gun , the +use pickup and the Physics Gun, and entities such as the crane.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This is the ideal function to move a physics shadow created with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/PhysicsInitShadow" title="Entity/PhysicsInitShadow">PhysicsInitShadow</a> or <a href="/page/Category_colon_Entity" title="Category:Entity">Entity</a>:<a href="/page/Entity/MakePhysicsObjectAShadow" title="Entity/MakePhysicsObjectAShadow">MakePhysicsObjectAShadow</a>.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
