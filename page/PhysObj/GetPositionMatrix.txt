<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the position and angle of the physics object as a 3x4 matrix (<a href="/page/Category_colon_VMatrix" title="Category:VMatrix">VMatrix</a> is 4x4 so the fourth row goes unused). The first three columns store the angle as a <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rotation_matrix">rotation matrix</a>, and the fourth column stores the position vector.
</p>
