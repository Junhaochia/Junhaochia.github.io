<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Calculates the linear and angular velocities on the center of mass for an offset force impulse. The outputs can be directly passed to <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a>:<a href="/page/PhysObj/AddVelocity" title="PhysObj/AddVelocity">AddVelocity</a> and <a href="/page/Category_colon_PhysObj" title="Category:PhysObj">PhysObj</a>:<a href="/page/PhysObj/AddAngleVelocity" title="PhysObj/AddAngleVelocity">AddAngleVelocity</a>, respectively.
</p>
