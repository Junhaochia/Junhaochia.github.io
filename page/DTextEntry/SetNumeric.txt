<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets whether or not to decline non-numeric characters as input.
</p><p>Numeric characters are 1234567890.-
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_boolean" title="Category:boolean">boolean</a> numericOnly</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
Whether to accept only numeric characters.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local TextEntry = vgui.Create( &quot;DTextEntry&quot; )
TextEntry:SetNumeric(true)</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">Only allow numeric characters.</div>
