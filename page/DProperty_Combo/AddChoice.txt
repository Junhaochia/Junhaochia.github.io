<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Set the "I am selected" option selected.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local choice = DP:CreateRow( &quot;Choices&quot;, &quot;Hello world&quot; )
choice:Setup( &quot;Combo&quot; )
choice:AddChoice( &quot;I am a choice&quot;, {} )
choice:AddChoice( &quot;I am selected&quot;, 8, true )
choice:AddChoice( &quot;I am not selected&quot;, &quot;Hello world&quot; )</code></pre>
