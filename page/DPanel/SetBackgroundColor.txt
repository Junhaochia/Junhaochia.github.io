<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets the background color of the panel.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_table" title="Category:table">table</a> color</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The background color.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates two empty panels with their background colors set to red and blue team colors.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">COLOR_TEAM_RED = Color(255, 64, 64, 255)
COLOR_TEAM_BLUE = Color(153, 204, 255, 255)

BGPanel1 = vgui.Create(&quot;DPanel&quot;)
BGPanel1:SetPos(20, 20)
BGPanel1:SetSize(200, 200)
BGPanel1:SetBackgroundColor(COLOR_TEAM_RED)
		
BGPanel2 = vgui.Create(&quot;DPanel&quot;)
BGPanel2:SetPos(220, 20)
BGPanel2:SetSize(200, 200)
BGPanel2:SetBackgroundColor(COLOR_TEAM_BLUE)</code></pre>
