<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Registers an ENT table with a classname. Reregistering an existing classname will automatically update the functions of all existing entities of that class.
</p>
<table class="gmodwiki_note bug_note">
<tr>
<td>BUG
</td>
<td>
<p>Sub-tables provided in the first argument will not carry over their metatable, and will receive a BaseClass key if the table was merged with the base's. Userdata references, which includes Vectors, Angles, Entities, etc. will not be copied.
</p><p><b>Pull Request: <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://github.com/Facepunch/garrysmod/pull/1300">#1300</a></b>
</p>
</td></tr></table>
