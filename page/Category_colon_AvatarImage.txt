<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelPage">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelPage" title="Form:PanelPage">PanelPage</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Used to show a player's Steam avatar. You are recommended but not limited to pick one of the following sizes: 16, 32, 64, 84, 128, 184.
</p>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates an AvatarImage with the LocalPlayer's avatar inside.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local Avatar = vgui.Create( &quot;AvatarImage&quot;, Panel )
Avatar:SetSize( 64, 64 )
Avatar:SetPos( 4, 4 )
Avatar:SetPlayer( LocalPlayer(), 64 )</code></pre>
