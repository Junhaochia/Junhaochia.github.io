<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Adds one or two items to the DForm.
If this method is called with only one argument, it is added to the bottom of the form. If two arguments are passed, they are placed side-by-side at the bottom of the form.
</p><p>Internally, this function is used by the various DForm functions to, for example, add labels to the left of buttons.
</p>
