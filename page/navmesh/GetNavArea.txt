<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns the Nav Area contained in this position that also satisfies the elevation limit.
</p><p>This function will properly see blocked <a href="/page/Category_colon_CNavArea" title="Category:CNavArea">CNavAreas</a>. See <a href="/page/Category_colon_navmesh" title="Category:navmesh">navmesh</a>.<a href="/page/navmesh/GetNearestNavArea" title="navmesh/GetNearestNavArea">GetNearestNavArea</a>.
</p>
