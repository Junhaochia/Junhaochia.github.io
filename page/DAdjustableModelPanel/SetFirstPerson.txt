<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Enables mouse and keyboard-based adjustment of the perspective.
</p><p>This is set to <i>true</i> automatically each time mouse capture is enabled, and hence doesn't serve as a usable setting, other than to disable this functionality after the <a href="/page/Category_colon_PANEL_Hooks" title="Category:PANEL Hooks">PANEL</a>:<a href="/page/PANEL/OnMousePressed" title="PANEL/OnMousePressed">OnMousePressed</a> event.
</p>
