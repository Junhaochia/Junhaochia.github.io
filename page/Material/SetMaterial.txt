<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets the material used by the panel.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>If the material is not showing up as the correct size, try setting the Material panel's <i>AutoSize</i> variable to <b>false</b>
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_string" title="Category:string">string</a> matname</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The file path of the material to set (relative to "garrysmod/materials/").</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates a Material panel and sets the material to a screen space effect.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">-- Black background panel
BGPanel = vgui.Create(&quot;DPanel&quot;)
BGPanel:SetSize(200, 200)
BGPanel:Center()
BGPanel:SetBackgroundColor(Color(0, 0, 0, 255))
		
local mat = vgui.Create(&quot;Material&quot;, BGPanel)
mat:SetPos(5, 5)
mat:SetSize(190, 190)

-- It's not really good to use a models material for VGUI drawing
-- But it looks good enough for a quick demo
mat:SetMaterial(&quot;models/screenspace&quot;)

-- Stretch to fit
mat.AutoSize = false</code></pre>
