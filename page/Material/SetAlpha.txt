<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelFunc">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelFunc" title="Form:PanelFunc">PanelFunc</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Sets the alpha value of the Material panel.
</p>
<h1 class="first_arg"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Arguments">Arguments</span></h1>
<div class="argument">
<p><span class="arg_number"></span> <span class="arg_chunk"><a href="/page/Category_colon_number" title="Category:number">number</a> alpha</span>
</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The alpha value, from 0 to 255.</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates a transparent SWEP icon in the middle of the screen.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local mat = vgui.Create(&quot;Material&quot;)
mat:SetSize(200, 200)
mat:Center()
mat:SetMaterial(&quot;weapons/swep&quot;)
	
mat:SetAlpha(128)</code></pre>
