<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelPage">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelPage" title="Form:PanelPage">PanelPage</a></span></span></h1>
<p>Choose a number from a number line, with zooming for precision. Zoom in by moving your mouse forward and moving back does the opposite. Looks like a blue circle until you click and hold on it
</p>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Create the Number Scratch with the minimum number being 0 and the max being 20
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local frame = vgui.Create( &quot;DFrame&quot; )
frame:SetSize( 300, 250 )
frame:Center()
frame:MakePopup()

local Scratch = vgui.Create( &quot;DNumberScratch&quot;, frame )
Scratch:SetPos( 50, 50 )
Scratch:SetValue( 5 )
Scratch:SetMin( 0 )
Scratch:SetMax( 20 )</code></pre>
