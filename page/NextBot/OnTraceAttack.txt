<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when a trace attack is done against the nextbot, allowing override of the damage being dealt by altering the <a href="/page/Category_colon_CTakeDamageInfo" title="Category:CTakeDamageInfo">CTakeDamageInfo</a>.
</p><p>This is called before <a href="/page/Category_colon_NEXTBOT_Hooks" title="Category:NEXTBOT Hooks">NEXTBOT</a>:<a href="/page/NEXTBOT/OnInjured" title="NEXTBOT/OnInjured">OnInjured</a>.
</p>
