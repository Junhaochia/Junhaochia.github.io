<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when the bot gets killed.
</p>

</p>
<div style="margin-left: 32px;">
The damage info</div>
</div>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Example of NPC becoming a ragdoll after death and sending death notification to everybodys killfeed.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function ENT:OnKilled( dmginfo )

	hook.Call( &quot;OnNPCKilled&quot;, GAMEMODE, self, dmginfo:GetAttacker(), dmginfo:GetInflictor() )

	self:BecomeRagdoll( dmginfo )

end</code></pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples_2">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example_2">Example</span></h2>
<p>Removes the body after 5 seconds, to prevent having lots of bodies laying around after a while.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">function ENT:OnKilled( dmginfo )
	
	hook.Call( &quot;OnNPCKilled&quot;, GAMEMODE, self, dmginfo:GetAttacker(), dmginfo:GetInflictor() )
	
	local body = ents.Create( &quot;prop_ragdoll&quot; )
	body:SetPos( self:GetPos() )
	body:SetModel( self:GetModel() )
	body:Spawn()
	
	self:Remove()
	
	timer.Simple( 5, function()
	
		body:Remove()
		
	end )

end</code></pre>
<p><b>Output:</b>
</p>
<div style="margin:2em;">The body disappears after 5 seconds.</div>
