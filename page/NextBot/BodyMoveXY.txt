<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Should only be called in BodyUpdate. This sets the move_x and move_y pose parameters of the bot to fit how they're currently moving, sets the animation speed to suit the ground speed, and calls FrameAdvance.
