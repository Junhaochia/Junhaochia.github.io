<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Sometimes, the value of 'Speed' can be different from getting the length calculated from 'OurOldVelocity', even though they should be the same, or close to same. It's recommended to do 'OurOldVelocity:Length()' instead.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">
ENT:PhysicsCollide( colData, collider )
    -- you may get two completely different values, and the second one should be more accurate.
    print(colData.Speed)
    print(colData.OurOldVelocity:Length())
end
</code></pre>
</td></tr></table>
