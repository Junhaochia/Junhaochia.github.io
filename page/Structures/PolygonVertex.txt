<div style="float:right;font-size:10px; font-weight: 100; margin: 0px 0px 10px 10px;"><a href="/page/Special:WhatLinksHere/Structures/PolygonVertex" title="Special:WhatLinksHere/Structures/PolygonVertex">What links here?</a></div>A structure containing vertex information for use with <a href="/page/Category_colon_surface" title="Category:surface">surface</a>.<a href="/page/surface/DrawPoly" title="surface/DrawPoly">DrawPoly</a>.
<p>UV coordinates describe which part of a given texture should be drawn at a vertex - your graphics card's interpolator will fill in space between vertices. UV coords range from 0-1, with 0 being top/left and 1 being bottom/right. If you're using a texture these are mandatory.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>You must pass a table of tables with this structure to the function. <b>You need to pass at least 3 points.</b>
</p>
</td></tr></table>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Your points must be defined in a <b>clockwise order.</b> Otherwise, your shape will not render.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
