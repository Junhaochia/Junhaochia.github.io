<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="DescriptionPanelPage">Description<span style="display: none;"><a href="/page/Form:PanelPage" title="Form:PanelPage">PanelPage</a></span></span></h1>
<p>A panel that draws a sprite on the player's HUD with the given <a href="/page/Category_colon_IMaterial" title="Category:IMaterial">IMaterial</a>, <a href="/page/Global/Color" title="Global/Color">Color</a> and rotation.
</p><p>A shortcut for this is <a href="/page/Global/CreateSprite" title="Global/CreateSprite">CreateSprite</a>().
</p>

<h1 class="first_example"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Examples">Examples</span></h1>
<h2 class="examples_number"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Example">Example</span></h2>
<p>Creates a DSprite with the <i>sent_ball</i> material, and sets the color to cyan.
</p>
<pre><code data-language="lua">local sprite = vgui.Create(&quot;DSprite&quot;)
sprite:SetMaterial(Material(&quot;sprites/sent_ball&quot;))
sprite:SetColor(Color(0, 255, 255))
sprite:Center()
sprite:SetSize(200, 200)</code></pre>
