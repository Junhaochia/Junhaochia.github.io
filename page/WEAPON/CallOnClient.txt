<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Calls a SWEP function on client.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note warning_note">
<tr>
<td>WARNING
</td>
<td>
<p>This uses the <a href="/page/Category_colon_usermessage" title="Category:usermessage">usermessage library</a> internally, because of that, the combined length of the arguments of this function may not exceed 254 bytes/characters or the function will cease to function!
</p>
</td></tr></table>
