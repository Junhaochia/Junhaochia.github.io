<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Allows you to modify viewmodel while the weapon in use before it is drawn. This hook only works if you haven't overridden <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PreDrawViewModel" title="GM/PreDrawViewModel">PreDrawViewModel</a>.
</p>
