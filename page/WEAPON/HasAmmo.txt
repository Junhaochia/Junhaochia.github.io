<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Returns whether the weapon has ammo left or not. It will return false when there's no ammo left in the magazine <b>and</b> when there's no reserve ammo left.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>This will return true for weapons like crowbar, gravity gun, etc.
</p>
</td></tr></table>
