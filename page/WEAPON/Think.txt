<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when the swep thinks.
</p><p>This hook won't be called during the deploy animation and when using <a href="/page/Category_colon_Weapon" title="Category:Weapon">Weapon</a>:<a href="/page/Weapon/DefaultReload" title="Weapon/DefaultReload">DefaultReload</a>.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Despite being a predicted hook, this hook is called clientside in single player, however it will not be recognized as a predicted hook to <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/GetCurrentCommand" title="Player/GetCurrentCommand">GetCurrentCommand</a>.

</td></tr></table>
