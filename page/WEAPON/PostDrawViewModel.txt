<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called after the view model has been drawn while the weapon in use. This hook is called from the default implementation of <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PostDrawViewModel" title="GM/PostDrawViewModel">PostDrawViewModel</a>, and as such, will not occur if it has been overridden.
</p><p><a href="/page/Category_colon_WEAPON_Hooks" title="Category:WEAPON Hooks">WEAPON</a>:<a href="/page/WEAPON/ViewModelDrawn" title="WEAPON/ViewModelDrawn">ViewModelDrawn</a> is an alternative hook which is always called before <a href="/page/Category_colon_GM_Hooks" title="Category:GM Hooks">GM</a>:<a href="/page/GM/PostDrawViewModel" title="GM/PostDrawViewModel">PostDrawViewModel</a>.
</p>
