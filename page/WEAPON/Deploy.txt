<h1> <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h1>
<p>Called when player has just switched to this weapon.
</p>
<table cellspacing="0" class="gmodwiki_note">
<tr>
<td>NOTE
</td>
<td>
<p>Due to this hook being predicted, it is not called clientside in singleplayer at all, and in multiplayer it will not be called clientside if the weapon is switched with <a href="/page/Category_colon_Player" title="Category:Player">Player</a>:<a href="/page/Player/SelectWeapon" title="Player/SelectWeapon">SelectWeapon</a> or the "use" console command, however it will be called clientside with the default weapon selection menu and when using <a href="/page/Category_colon_CUserCmd" title="Category:CUserCmd">CUserCmd</a>:<a href="/page/CUserCmd/SelectWeapon" title="CUserCmd/SelectWeapon">SelectWeapon</a>
</p>
</td></tr></table>
